# MH Markets Knowledge Base

## Company Overview

### MH Markets Overview
MH Markets is a multi-asset online broker offering CFD and Forex trading across forex, indices, stocks, commodities, and metals. The company presents itself as a globally regulated and licensed broker and provides access through web-based and MetaTrader trading platforms.

### MH Markets Positioning
MH Markets positions itself as an online trading provider focused on market access, trading technology, educational resources, and customer support.

## Product Categories

### Product Category: Forex

## Account Types

### Account Type: Standard
The Standard account is described as suitable for regular trading and requires a USD 50 first deposit.

#### Standard Account Features
- low spread
- MetaTrader 4 access
- MetaTrader 5 access
- leverage up to 1:2000
- stop-out level of 30%

### Account Type: ECN
The ECN account is described as professional-grade trading and requires a USD 1,000 first deposit.

#### ECN Account Features
- ultra-low spread
- MetaTrader 4 access
- MetaTrader 5 access
- leverage up to 1:2000
- stop-out level of 30%

### Account Type: Prime
The Prime account is presented as a specialized account for high-volume traders and requires a USD 100 first deposit.

#### Prime Account Features

- MetaTrader 4 access
- MetaTrader 5 access
- leverage up to 1:2000
- stop-out level of 30%
