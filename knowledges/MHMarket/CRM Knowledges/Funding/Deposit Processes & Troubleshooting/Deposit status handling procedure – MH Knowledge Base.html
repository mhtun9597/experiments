    Deposit status handling procedure

    Jason
    April 16, 2026 06:58 Updated

    This article outlines the procedures for Customer Support (CS) to handle various deposit-related issues. It provides guidance on identifying the cause, coordinating with the Funding Team, and ensuring proper communication and resolution for clients.

     

    Purpose

    The purpose of this SOP is to ensure that all deposit-related cases are handled consistently, accurately, and efficiently by Customer Support. It also aims to maintain compliance with company policies, particularly the strict prohibition of third-party deposits.

     

    Scope

    This SOP applies to all Customer Support staff responsible for handling deposit inquiries and issues. It covers interactions with clients, coordination with the Funding Team, and adherence to internal processes for deposit verification, processing, and refunds.

     

    The Process Flowchart

     

     

    Step 1: CS User verification 

    CS must identify the presents of green tick (authorized user) on Zendesk before providing any personal information to the client. 
    If the client reached out on live chat without green-tick, CS must advice the client to open a new live chat window once login to the client portal or send us an email from the registered email address. 

     

    Step 2: CS Identify the request

    CS need to make sure to fully understand the request from client and/or Account Manager before further assisting them. 
    Useful macro: "Sorry I didn't fully understand your issue/concern, could you kindly elaborate more?" 

     

    Step 3: CS Check deposit status in CRM

    CS are required to check in CRM for all deposit status check request to confirm the details.
    The detailed CRM guide on how to check the deposit status can be found CRM 1.0 // CRM 2.0. 

    • Client may deposit anytime 24/7, however our Funding team operating hour is from Mon - Fri 04:00 - 18:00 (GMT+3). Any delayed deposits that require manual process may subject to the Funding team operation hour
    • CS should aware that all deposit methods are supposed to be processed instantly, except bank wire transfer deposit. which may take up to 4 business days. However, even with instant deposit, after the deposit credited into client's trading account, it may take 1-2 minutes for the funds to reflect in the MT platform.
    • If there is no record in CRM, but client provided proof of payment - Escalate to Funding team

     

    Step 4: CSR request Proof of Payment

    • CSR should ask the client to provide proof of deposit and provide appropriate respond according to the situation
    • Proof of Payment can be:
      • Screenshot showing the funds has been deducted from bank account
      • Crypto transaction screenshot and the Hash ID/TxID in TEXT form
      • Bank Wire Transfer receipt 
      • E-wallet transaction record 

    Suggest using Zendesk Macro 3231. Request for Deposit Screenshot: Could you please provide us the transaction screenshot, where the Transaction no / transaction hash ( Txid ) and other details are clearly visible. 

     

    Situation 1: Deposit status Finished

    Situation 1.1 Amount match
    • If the full amount has been credited to the account, CSR should advise the client to check the trading account or wallet where the funds were credited.
    • If deposited into User Wallet or wrong trading account, CS agent may advise them to perform internal transfer to the correct account. 
    Situation 1.2 Full Amount Not Credited to the Account - Amount mismatch
    • If the full amount has not been credited to the account due to a mismatch where the CRM amount is less than the proof of payment. > Escalate to Funding team 
    • CSR to change the ticket tag to Delay - client error 
     Situation 1.3 Full Amount Not Credited to the Account (Reset negative balance)
    • If amount in CRM matches the amount shown in the proof of payment, but the amount to the account is less > it was deducted towards negative balance.
    • Change the ticket to theme to negative balance reset and change the status On- hold
    • Escalate to the Operations team via JIRA and include the issue, trading account, and server details.
    • OP team will take the necessary action to resolve the negative balance and re-credit the full deposit amount
    • OP team to notify CS upon successful resolution
    • CSR to inform the client that the concern has been completed and mark the ticket as solved.

    Situation 2: Deposit status Reject

    CSR may need to check the CRM to determine the reason for the rejection.

    • If the client does not respond to the deposit confirmation within 2–3 days, the deposit request will be rejected.
    • If the client did not transfer the funds according to the deposit application, the Funding Team will reject the transaction.
    • The CSR should inform the client of the reason for the deposit rejection accordingly.

    • The CSR should tag the ticket as “Funding Guidance” and mark it as Solved

       

    Situation 3: Deposit status Pending or no Record Found

    • If the receipt name matches the name in CRM > 
      • CSR Escalate to the Funding Team for further checking and put the ticket to On Hold.
         
    • If the name does not match the CRM due > to a third-party deposit, 
      • CSR change the ticket theme to “3rd Party Deposit" 
      • CSR should explain that third-party deposits are not allowed and collect the client’s bank details for the refund.
      • CSR should also inform the client that the refund must be processed back to the same account or wallet used for the original deposit, where applicable.
      • CSR Escalate to the Funding Team for further checking and put the ticket to On Hold.
    For Customer Support attention:

    Please note that the CS process concludes once the Funding team has completed processing and provided their resolution. Please scroll down below to view the Funding team workflow.

    CSR must keep the ticket on hold until the Funding Team provides the receipt or proof of refund

     

    Funding Team Workflows

    Step 1: Funding team starts processing

    The funding team to Add an internal note to Zendesk ticket: Checking"

    Step 2: Funding Team to identify the Deposit type
    • If Equal Money or OpenPAYD > escalate to Finance Team to check the bank app if funds received.
    • if Mashreq - Funding team will directly check if funds has been received
    • If funds have been found > go to Step X - notify CS.
    • If funds have not been received > return to CS to request additional proof of payment.

    Step 3: FD Deposit type = Other

    • if the payment method is not bank wire, FD team to contact PSP provider through Telegram and share payment proof
    • If PSP have located the funds > they will send a callback. Upon receiving it, CRM will automatically credited funds. DS team proceed to notify CS > Steps X
    • If the funds have not been located > return to CS to request additional proof of payment.

    Step 4: FD Automatic processing fails

    • If the funds have not been credited automatically upon the callback > FD team to check with Check with Finance team and with Channel technician team to identify the issue.

    • If the funds have been confirmed > manually process the deposit. notify CS > Steps X

    Step 5: FD team to notify the CSR

    • Add internal note with the delay reason and to notify the CSR that the concern has been completed:
    •  Macro FD122. Deposit Delay - Client error
    • Macro FD123. Deposit Delay - Internal
    • Macro FD124. Deposit Delay - PSP

    Step 5: CSR notify the client

    Upon receiving ticket back from FD, CS will update the ticket theme and notify the client on resolution 

    • CSR should also inform the client that the refund must be processed back to the same account or wallet used for the original deposit, where applicable.
    • If the resolution includes refund, CS to provide client with the details and the proof of refund and explain when client will receive funds, set expectations, and solve the ticket. 

    CSR to inform the client the refund may take approximately by PSP: 

    • Bank wire: 1-4 working days
    • Visa/Master:  4-15 working days
    • Remittance payment (Bank Transfer): 2-3 hours or 24 hours
    • E wallets:  2-3 hours or 24 hours

       

    Important thing to keep in mind about Deposit process 

    1. Client may deposit anytime 24/7, however our Funding team operating hour is from Mon - Fri 04:00 - 18:00 (GMT+3). Any delayed deposits that require manual process may subject to the Funding team operation hour.  
      *The daily exchange rate will be announced in Teams > Payment Channel Status > Daily Conversion Rate group every morning. (If you notice any discrepancies between the system rate in CRM and the announced rate, please inform Funding team.) 
       
    2. We offer multiple payment methods and channels to clients. The available options may vary depending on the channel’s minimum deposit requirement and operating hours.
      If a client reports that they are unable to find or use a specific deposit channel, always check in the CRM Deposit Group first before escalate to Funding team. 
       
    3. Furthermore, clients residing in different region/countries will be assigned to different deposit group. Detailed information can be found in Payment Method Rename Excel.
       
    4. Always advise client to fully read and understand the deposit instruction on the right side of the page when performing a deposit. 
       
    5. For VISA/Mastercard Credit/Debit card deposits, clients should provide a color copy of both the front and back sides of their card, ensuring the following details are visible:
      i. Full name of the cardholder
      ii. Last 4 digits of your card number
      iii. Card expiry date
      Security Recommendations
      For security purposes, all digits except the last 4 on the front side of the card should be covered.
      The 3-digit CVV code on the back of the card must be covered (we do not require the CVV code).
      All other required details must be clear and visible.
      **Mandatory to verify card details when exceed a total deposit of 30K USD.
       
    6. MH Markets does not charge fees for deposits or withdrawals. However, please be aware that international banks may impose fees independently. MH Markets bears no responsibility for such charges. The client is responsible for the following fees:
      i. Bank processing fee for Global Bank Wire Transfer
      ii. Gas Fee for Cryptocurrency
       
    7. More related information, please refer Deposit Policy.

     

    Funding team investigation process (INTERNAL USAGE ONLY)

    • The Funding Team will always recheck the status in the CRM upon receiving a ticket escalation from Customer Support (CS).

      1. If the deposit has already been completed by the time the Funding Team reviews the ticket, they will update the status directly in the Internal Note. 
      2. If the deposit is still pending, the Funding Team will reply “Checking” in the Internal Note and liaise with the Payment Service Provider (PSP) via Telegram to investigate the status.
      3. If no deposit record in CRM, the Funding team will refer to the deposit receipt or proof from the client and identify which payment method client used. 
         
    • Funding team will provide a further update once: 
      1. The deposit has been processed, or
      2. Additional information is required from the client.
         

    • When the Internal Note shows “Checking,” CS may advise the client to wait while the relevant team is conducting further investigation.
       

    • The Payment Service Provider (PSP) will investigate and identify any abnormalities that may have prevented the deposit from being processed instantly. Possible outcomes include:

      1. PSP Approved 
        • PSP callback the deposit order, and payment is successfully processed
        • CRM will automatically credit the funds into the client's trading account (If no, Funding team will process manually) 
      2. PSP pending Further Information 
        • Funding team will update what information are required in Internal Note. 
        • CS will request the required information from the client
        • This process may continue back and forth until the deposit is completed. (MUST keep the ticket on-hold until resolved)
      3. PSP Rejected
        • The Funding Team will request the client’s bank account or crypto wallet details to process a refund.
        • The refund must be made to the same account or wallet used for the original deposit, where applicable.   
        • In cases where the client deposit through crypto exchange instead of personal crypto wallet, we are unable to refund it to the crypto exchange. So Funding team will request for a personal crypto wallet address with the same network to process the refund. > 
           
    • For no deposit record in CRM case, Funding team will forward the deposit receipt or proof from the client to the PSP to decide if they can process the transaction. (Except Bank Wire Transfer payment method)
      1. PSP can process (depends on PSP policy)
        • Funding team may request client to create a new deposit order. The client just need to create the order, no need to deposit again. The funds will auto credit once client create new deposit order. (*Usually when using local bank transfer)
        • Or Funding team will create the deposit order for the client and credit the funds manually. (*Usually when using crypto)
      2. PSP cannot process 
        • PSP will require client's bank account details to refund. 
        • Follow the same process as described in section 2.1, point number 3.
      3. Bank Wire Transfer 
        • For bank wire transfer deposits, Funding Team may seek assistance from Finance Team to check our bank account and verify whether the client’s deposit has been received (If the deposit was not made to Mashreq Bank).

        • If received, Funding team will manually create the deposit order for the client and process the deposit.
