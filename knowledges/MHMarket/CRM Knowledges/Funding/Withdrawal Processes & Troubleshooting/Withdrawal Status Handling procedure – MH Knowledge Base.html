    Withdrawal Status Handling procedure

    Jason
    April 10, 2026 11:47 Updated

    This article provides Customer Support (CS) with clear guidance on the standard procedures for handling withdrawal status inquiries, ensuring accurate, consistent, and timely communication with clients.

    Scope

    This SOP outlines the step-by-step process for checking withdrawal status and providing appropriate responses to related inquiries, including communication guidelines and escalation procedures where necessary.

    The Process Flowchart

     

    Step 1: User verification 

    CS must identify the presents of green-tick (authorized user) on Zendesk before providing any personal information to the client. 
    If the client reached out on live chat without green-tick, CS must advice the client to open a new live chat window once login to the client portal, or send us an email from the registered email address. 

    Step 2: Identify the request

    CS need to make sure to fully understand the request from client and/or Account Manager before further assisting them. 
    Useful macro: "Sorry I didn't fully understand your issue/concern, could you kindly elaborate more?" 

    Step 3: Check withdrawal status in CRM

    CS are required to check in CRM for all withdrawal status check request to confirm the details.
    The detailed CRM guide on how to check the withdrawal status can be found CRM1.0 // CRM2.0 

    Step 4: Appropriate respond according to the situation

    Situation 1: Withdrawal request within 24 business hours
    • If the withdrawal request is within the SLA (in Processing status and within 1-2 hours) or (in Remittance in Progress status). CS may explain directly to client the expected timeframe and advise them to wait patiently until the full 24 hours have passed. 

    Zendesk Macro 132: Withdrawal Processing Time:

    "Generally, our finance department processes withdrawal requests within one business day. However, we make every effort to expedite the process, and the actual processing time is typically shorter (usually completed within 2-4 hours)." 

    • Record the ticket theme as "Withdrawal Status", only escalate to Funding team if the client requests to speed up the process. Otherwise, CS can proceed with asking if client require any further assistance. 
        Situation 2: Pending confirmation from client
    • If the withdrawal request is pending for client to reply/action the "Email Confirmation" to process, please advise client to check their registered email address mailbox for an email subject "xxxx".
       
    • CS can check the status in CRM for the subject. It will show ticked when client confirms. 
      Screenshot example (INTERNAL USAGE ONLY): 

    Useful macro 136: Withdrawal Status (Pending email confirmation) 出金进度（待确认邮件）

    "Thank you for your patience, kindly be informed that we are pending for your reply and confirmation on the email subject [insert email subject]. You may check your registered email address mailbox for the details. Feel free to reach out to us again if you require further assistance."

    • Record the ticket theme as "WD Additional Verification" and wait for the client's reply. If no reply after the standard waiting period, CS can proceed to ending chat procedure. 
       
    • If the client feedback unable to click the confirmation button in the email, CS may advice them to directly reply on the email itself.
       
    • Once the client replied/confirmed the email, CS may proceed escalate to Funding team to help verify and process. 
       
    • Possible additional verification required including but not limited to: 
      • Complete Withdrawal Verification KYC Level, usually will have a link in the email
      • Screen recording showing the Crypto wallet address together with client's name
      • Confirmation of the processing/handling fee to proceed withdrawal 
      • Confirm the withdrawal funds by oneself (AM), require AM to reply
        Situation 3: Withdrawal request passed 24 business hours
    • If the withdrawal request has passed the 24 business hours expected timeframe, CS are required to escalate the ticket to Funding team.  

    Useful macro 137: Withdrawal Delayed 出金延误 

    "Your withdrawal request is currently still pending further review. We have informed the relevant team to expedite the process and will update you as soon as it’s completed.
    Thank you for your understanding." 

    • Record the ticket theme as "WD delayed Internally", and keep the ticket open/on-hold until Funding team reply. 
       
    • Update and follow up the request according to Funding team's reply. May keep the ticket open/on-hold until fully resolved. 
       
    • Possible reason where withdrawal request still in pending after 24 business hours: 
      • Withdrawal being place on hold by Risk Control (RC) team 
      • No trading history between deposit and withdrawal. Funding team will process the initial review 24hours after the request was submitted. 
        Situation 4: Withdrawal status remitted, client did not receive the funds 
    • If the withdrawal status is showing Remitted in the CRM, but the client feedback they has not received the funds (passed the SLA specified for the payment method). 
       
    • Record the ticket theme as "WD delayed by PM", ask the client to provide supporting documents/proof the funds has not been received. Such as: 
      • a bank statement covering the date the funds were sent, or
      • a screenshot of their bank/e-wallet account transaction history
         
    • Escalate the request to Funding team and keep the ticket open/on-hold until Funding team reply. 
       
    • Funding team will usually provide the remittance receipt, CS may provide the receipt to client for them to further check. 
       
    • Possible reason client did not receive the funds:
      i. Transaction was rejected by client's bank
      ii. Transaction was rejected by our PSP (payment service provider)
      In these cases, the funds will reverse back to client's trading account, and Funding team will suggest alternative option to withdraw. 
       
    • Update and follow up the request according to Funding team's reply. May keep the ticket open/on-hold until fully resolved. 

    Step 5: Escalation to Funding team (only when required)

    • Do take note Funding team handles the initial review and deduction review. Risk control review is handle by RC team. 
      If the status is in pending under Risk control review, please check ticket history if the client has any ongoing case from RC. 
       
    • CS need to use the macro 211. Escalation to Funding team, and summarize a clear and short description of the issue in the internal note.

    Important notes before escalating to Funding team

    1. All withdrawal requests are processed within 24 business hours, and our business hours are from Monday to Friday 09:00 AM to 11:00 PM (GMT+8). Any withdrawal requests submitted after 11:00 PM will be processed on the next business day.
       
    2. All withdrawal requests are required to go through 3 internal process before the funds are remitted to client. 
      i. Internal review - Initial review by the Funding team. 
      ii. Risk control review - Following review by the Risk Control team. 
      iii. Deduction review - Final review by Funding team before deducting the funds (Only when the Deduction upon application column in CRM is "NO")
       
    3. In most cases, once a withdrawal request is successfully submitted, the system will automatically deduct the funds. The only exception is when the trading account contains Credit.
       
    4. Different withdrawal payment method will have different expected remittance time. Please see the expected remittance times for each payment method below: 
    1. Deposited funds must be withdrawn using the same payment method as the original deposit. Withdrawals requests via a different method may be subject to additional processing fees. 
      Profits however can be withdrawn using any of the available withdrawal methods.
       
    2. More related information, please refer Withdrawal Policy.

    Exceptional Cases – Escalation Allowed Even Within SLA

    Normally, CS should wait until the standard timeframe ends. However, escalation within SLA is allowed in exceptional scenarios such as:

    • High‑tier / VIP clients
    • Escalation requested by Account Manager/Sales (AM)
    • Repeated follow up on the same withdrawal request from the client
    • Previous unresolved withdrawal issues
    • Client reports urgent hardship scenarios

    In these cases, escalate early and note clearly why the escalation is outside the standard rule.
