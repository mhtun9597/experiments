    Withdrawal Issue 

    How long do I need to wait before withdrawing my money?

    • Submission Time: Clients can submit withdrawal requests at any time of day. However, withdrawal processing time is Monday to Friday, 09:00 AM to 11:00 PM (GMT+8). Any withdrawal requests submitted after 11:00PM will be processed on the next business day.
    • Trading Activity Requirement: For accounts with no trading or minimal trading activity, withdrawal requests must wait a full 24 hours in the account. Additionally, we need to verify whether the deposits and withdrawals originate from the same source. If there are any special circumstances, please provide an explanation via email.

    Is there a limit on withdrawing funds?

    • Minimum Withdrawal Amounts:
      • Non-Chinese Regions: The minimum withdrawal amount is $30 USD.
      • Chinese Regions: The minimum withdrawal amount is slightly higher at $150 USD.
      • Payment Channels: Some payment channels may impose their own minimum withdrawal requirements. For example, MyFatoorah sets a minimum withdrawal of $50 USD. Therefore, if using MyFatoorah, the minimum withdrawal amount will be $50 USD.
    • Maximum Withdrawal Amounts: The maximum withdrawal amount depends on the specific limits set by each payment channel. Different payment channels may have different maximum limits.
      • Large Amounts: For large amounts, especially in CNY, some channels may require separate payments. In such cases, it’s important to confirm with the client whether multiple remittances are acceptable. If multiple remittances could potentially cause the client’s bank card to be frozen, the client should decide if they are comfortable with that risk. If not, they should wait until the entire amount can be processed in one transaction.

    What are the possible reasons for a withdrawal to be in a pending status?

    • Security Review: All withdrawals are subject to a security review to ensure the safety of user funds. This may involve additional checks and verifications to prevent unauthorized access or potentially fraudulent activities.
    • Compliance Review: To comply with regulatory requirements, Risk Control needs to conduct compliance reviews on each withdrawal. This may include Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures or other verification processes to ensure the withdrawal complies with applicable regulations. The time required for a compliance review can vary depending on the complexity of the review and regulatory requirements.
    • Waiting for Confirmation from Clients/Sales:
      • First-Time Withdrawal: For the first withdrawal using USDT, confirmation of the withdrawal address is necessary. Sales and the client should coordinate to ensure accuracy.
      • Withdrawal Fees: If the client exceeds five withdrawals in a month, or if the withdrawal and deposit methods do not match, or for daily RMB withdrawals, additional information may be required. Please check the withdrawal fees for details.
      • CP, PAMM Accounts: Withdrawals involving credit accounts, CP accounts, or PAMM accounts need confirmation before proceeding and must be confirmed with the client.
      • Credit- When the system identifies that an account holds credit, a credit confirmation notification will be triggered. Depending on the specific terms and conditions of various promotions, certain credit funds may be restricted from profit withdrawal, while others may remain eligible
      • Multiple Submissions by Clients: If clients submit multiple withdrawal requests, communicate with them to clarify whether to process all requests.
      • Risk of Liquidation: If the client has open positions and the Free Margin is below 200%, there is a risk of liquidation during withdrawal. With Risk of Liquidation notifications.
      • Withdrawal with No Trading Activity: Withdrawals with no trading activity must wait for a full 24 hours in the account. Additionally, verify whether the deposit and withdrawal methods match. If there are any special circumstances, please provide an explanation via email.
    • Technical Issues: In rare cases, technical issues or glitches may cause withdrawals to be pending. We will work to resolve technical issues as quickly as possible.

    Why was my withdrawal unsuccessful?

    • Third-Party Withdrawals: We do not accept payments to third parties. The recipient's name must match the name registered with MHMarkets.
    • Insufficient Margin: If the account does not have enough margin (available funds), the withdrawal request may be declined.
    • Insufficient Balance: If the account balance is insufficient to cover the requested withdrawal amount, the request will not proceed.
    • Incorrect Bank Information: Double-check your bank information! Incorrect account numbers, wrong branch codes, or misspelled bank names can prevent successful withdrawals. Accuracy is crucial.
    • Incorrect Withdrawal Currency: If you are using a local bank account, ensure that you use the local currency. Using the wrong currency will result in rejection.
    • Incorrect USDT Address: If dealing with USDT (Tether), ensure the address is correct. Even a single digit error in the crypto address can cause the transaction to fail.

     

    Why Do I Need to Provide Proof of Deposit?

    Providing proof of deposit is crucial for several reasons:

    1. Identity Confirmation: When you deposit funds into your account, the platform needs to verify that the transaction is genuinely from you. Proof of deposit, such as a screenshot showing your name, the validity period, and card number, acts as your confirmation, ensuring that it’s really you behind the transaction.
    2. Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Measures: Financial institutions are strict about AML regulations to prevent money laundering, fraud, and other illicit activities. Your deposit proof helps confirm that the transaction complies with these regulations and is legitimate.
    3. Security and Compliance: Proof of deposit acts as a security measure to safeguard financial transactions and uphold industry standards. It helps in preventing unauthorized access and ensures that your money is protected from any fraudulent activity.
    4. Peace of Mind (Yours and Theirs): Seeing confirmation that your deposit is verified provides reassurance. It indicates that your funds are safe, and the platform is committed to protecting your assets and providing reliable service.

     

    Why Do We Ask for a Reason When You Withdraw?

    Providing a reason for your withdrawal helps us in several ways:

    1. Understanding Our Clients: By understanding the reason behind your withdrawal, we gain insight into your financial needs and preferences. This helps us better tailor our services to meet your needs.
    2. Client Loss Prevention: If we observe a pattern, such as many clients withdrawing funds to switch to other service providers, we can address the issue proactively. This information helps us improve our service and prevent potential client loss.
