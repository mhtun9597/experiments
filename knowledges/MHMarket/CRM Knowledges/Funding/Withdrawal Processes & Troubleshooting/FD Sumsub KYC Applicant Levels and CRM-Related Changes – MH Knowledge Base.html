    FD Sumsub KYC Applicant Levels and CRM-Related Changes

    Arion
    February 10, 2026 09:24 Updated

    There are 2 conditions that will trigger Enhanced KYC for a withdrawal of funds: 

    • If a client changes their registered email, AND within 30 days, they also change their USDT withdrawal address > the FD EDD check will be triggered.
    • If a client links a new USDT address in the Client Portal, AND the same address is detected in the CRM as being used by other client > the USDT Withdrawal Selfie step will be triggered.

    Once triggered, the client must complete the KYC process and provide documents as outlined below:

    There are also a way to triggew Enhanced EDD KYC check manually upon suspicions from Risk: 

    Manual USDT Withdrawal Selfie Step

    The Manual USDT Withdrawal is triggered by the FD and an email is sent 

    If the system is unable to automatically detect the necessary verification elements, an email will be sent requesting the client to manually complete the USDT Withdrawal Selfie step.

    Auto USDT Withdrawal Selfie Step (System Auto-Detection – Already Live in CRM)

    When a client links a USDT address via the Client Portal, and that address is detected in CRM as being shared with other client accounts, this KYC condition will be triggered.
    In this case, the client must complete KYC in the Client Portal by submitting:

    • Proof of Identity (POI)
    • Liveness Selfie
    Note
    If any of the above KYC levels are triggered and not completed, the client will be restricted from performing the following actions:
    • Internal transfer requests
    • Same-name account applications
    • Withdrawal requests

    Withdrawal Verification (To Be Released)

    A new Withdrawal Verification feature will be launched in the future.
    Currently, for clients who deposit via UnionPay and attempt to withdraw via USDT, an FD team member will manually initiate email-based KYC verification during the withdrawal review process.

    CRM Interface: Audit Management > KYC Recheck

    • In CRM go to Client Management > EDD KYC Users

    • You can add filter Trigger Rule > FD EDD Check
