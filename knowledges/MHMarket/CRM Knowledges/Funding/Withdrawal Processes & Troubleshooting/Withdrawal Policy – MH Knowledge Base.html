    Withdrawal Policy

    Arion
    April 13, 2026 09:09 Updated

    In this article described all withdrawal Rules and Policies, such as: 

    • Withdrawal Processing Time
    • Withdrawal limit
    • Withdrawal Fees
    • General Withdrawal rules and the requirements
    • Region specific withdrawal rules
    • Temporary Restriction After Email or Password Change
    • Digital Currency Withdrawal

    Withdrawal Processing Time

    Client Information: Withdrawal processing time is Monday to Friday, 09:00 AM to 11:00 PM (GMT+8). 

    Any withdrawal requests submitted after 11:00 PM will be processed on the next business day. 

    The time it takes for funds to be credited to the account depends on the banking situation of the account provided by the client. (except in special circumstances)

    • *If the payment channel has a higher threshold, that channel’s limit applies (e.g., AED requires USD 50).
      If the Trading account clients wish to withdraw the entire balance but it doesn’t meet the minimum, they may deposit the difference and then submit a withdrawal request. But Commission account withdrawals are only processed once the minimum amount is met.

    • **Due to payment channel limitations, the minimum withdrawal amount for SGD is 2,000 SGD.
      For requests below this threshold, clients are advised to withdraw via USDT instead.
      If the client insists on withdrawing in SGD or does not have a USDT address, a handling fee of 30 SGD will be charged. 

    Withdrawal Fees

    • Different USD Wallet Withdrawal Rule - For USDT withdrawals, if the deposit and withdrawal are done using different network types (e.g., deposit  withTRC20 and withdrawal with BEP20), an additional 2 USD fee will be charged. 

      This is considered a non-standard operation, and in order to verify fund ownership and the authenticity of the request, client need to provide a screenshot showing the USDT address along with client's name for our review through their registered email. 

      Additionally, confirmation from the finance department is required to ensure it can be processed.

    • A 5% fee will be charged if the deposit and withdrawal methods are inconsistent.
    • For Myanmar clients, a 2% fee will be charged if there is no trading after the deposit.
    • For multiple method deposits, if the withdrawal amount exceeds the internal transfer funds of the commission, a 5% fee will be charged. If multiple deposit methods are used for an account, withdrawals from commission accounts can be made using any method. For other accounts, withdrawals can only be made after depositing the corresponding principal amount according to the deposit method, with the remaining amount considered as profit and available for the client to choose their withdrawal method.

    General Withdrawal rules and the requirements

    1. Withdrawals to third parties (3rd party withdrawal) are not accepted. The name of the recipient must match the name and account name registered with MH Markets.  
    2. The currencies for deposits and withdrawals must be consistent. (Withdrawals will be reviewed according to the rules if the deposit and withdrawal methods are different.)  
    3. Withdrawals from a cent account must be requested in USD.

    4. If the client has open positions, as the system detected the margin level below 200% (excluding any credit balance), a Withdrawal cannot be submitted. 

    5. If the withdraw account with a credit, a pop-up window will appear and request the client to confirm the credit deduction. Client needs to click "Ok" to agree the confirmation of credit deduction and proceed the withdraw application otherwise then click Cancel, so then the application will be terminal. If the account does not meet the relevant promotion terms and conditions requirement, the credit will be deducted before processing the withdrawal. 
    6. Account under Social Trading: Due to the automatic deduction feature of profit sharing, if the reserved balance is low, a pop-up will appear with a Profit Share distribution confirmation. Client need to click Ok to agree the confirmation of credit deduction and proceed the withdraw application otherwise then click Cancel, the application will be terminal.
    7. If the account has joined the PAMM or CP, must check with the operation team that whether it needs to disconnect. If it is necessary, we will then confirm with the belonging sales whether to continue the execution of the withdrawal.
    8. For using Visa or MasterCard (MyFatoorah(AED)), withdrawals are only applicable to clients who deposited using this channel. In addition, Any deposits made via Visa/MasterCard must first be withdrawn back to the same card, up to the original deposit amount. Profits and commissions can then be withdrawn through other available methods such as USDT.

    Region specific withdrawal rules

    • For Myanmar region, withdrawals are limited to USD currency. For Myanmar U trading accounts, USDT deposits and withdrawals are processed directly through the backend, with no currency conversion issues. 
    • For Thailand region: For clients who deposit via cryptocurrency, KYC verification is required each time a withdrawal is requested to a NEW cryptocurrency address. The client is required to upload Identity document and Selfie
    • CNY is not supported for 中国工商银行(Industrial and Commercial Bank of China (ICBC). (For special reasons, please confirm with the finance department.)  

    Temporary Restriction After Email or Password Change

    To strengthen account security, if a client changes their email or password, the system will automatically restrict withdrawals and internal transfers to different name accounts for 24 hours.

    However, if the respective sales confirms the request was made by the client, Funding may manually lift the restriction.

    Digital Currency Withdrawal

    • For any initial USDT withdrawal or to a new USDT address, it requires to make a confirmation directly within the Secondary Confirmation of USDT Address email from withdrawal@mhmarkets.com. Client needs to click "Confirm Withdrawal" to proceed the withdraw application otherwise the application will be terminal.
    • For withdrawal amounts of $1,000 USD or more, the system will send the email confirmation to the respective account manager automatically that to confirm with sales whether the request was made by the client. 
    • After the finance department processes the remittance, it typically takes about 24 hours for the system to confirm the successful export of funds and validate it automatically. During the verification period (buffer period), if the client submits a withdrawal request to the same USDT address, they will receive a second confirmation email from the system for the USDT withdrawal address. 
    • Verified USDT addresses will display a green checkmark on the withdrawal review page. 

    • Enhanced Due Diligence Requirements
      To ensure compliance with regulatory standards and protect the integrity of our platform, additional Know Your Customer (KYC) verification may be required under certain withdrawal conditions. These enhanced due diligence checks include identity verification (POI) and a real-time selfie check.

      KYC verification will be triggered if any of the following activities are detected during USDT withdrawals:

      - A single wallet address is being used by multiple user accounts.

      - Frequent or unusual changes to wallet addresses.

      Please note:
      Clients under the Jackie team are required to complete KYC verification for all withdrawal methods, regardless of the above conditions.

    MMK account:

    Withdrawals must be processed according to the exchange rate announced in the group on the day of the withdrawal. The exchange rate needs to be manually updated before processing the withdrawal. 

    *(formula: the withdrawal amount (MMK) platform exchange rate (2400)/ daily buying rate =the actual  withdrawal USDT amount

    *MMK commission accounts are currently not supported 

     

    Once the finance department clicks "Return," the withdrawal order will be restored to a modifiable state on the withdrawal deduction review page.
