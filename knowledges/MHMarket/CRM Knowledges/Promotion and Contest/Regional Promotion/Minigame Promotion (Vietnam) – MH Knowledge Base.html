    Minigame Promotion (Vietnam)

    Customer Assistant
    April 15, 2026 10:23 Updated

    The promotion targets new Vietnamese clients with Standard accounts trading XAU/USD from April 15 to May 1, 2026. Rewards are given to the first 30 clients meeting tiered net deposit and trading volume criteria, with tiers offering notebooks, pens, raincoats, and bottles. Eligibility requires registration via the official Vietnam fanpage inbox, and final rewards depend on end-of-campaign account status. Only one reward per client is allowed, based on the highest tier achieved.

    Campaign Period

    15 April - 1 May 2026 (Server Time)
    Or until all rewards are fully distributed

    Eligibility

    • New Vietnam clients only
    • Standard accounts only
    • Applicable product: XAU/USD (Metals)
    • Registration via official Vietnam fanpage inbox is required

    Reward Structure and Mechanism

    All new Vietnamese clients with Standard accounts are eligible to participate in this promotion. Clients may qualify for one of three tiers based on their net deposit amount and total trading volume.

    For example, if a client makes a net deposit of USD 200 and trades 0.2 lots of a qualified instrument, the client’s name will receive a “qualification stamp.” Rewards will be granted to the first 30 clients who meet the qualification criteria.

    For Example:

    A client achieves a net deposit of $200 and trades 0.2 lot XAU/USD during the
    Promotion period.
    ✅ Eligible for Tier 2 reward

    A client meets the requirements but withdraws funds before the end of the Promotion,
    causing net deposit to fall below $200.
    ❌ Not eligible for reward

    A client qualifies for Tier 2 early, then withdraws funds, reducing Net Deposit, and later
    deposits again to re-qualify. The system records a new qualification timestamp
    ❌ The client may lose priority and may no longer receive the reward

    • One client can receive only one reward (highest tier achieved)
    • Rewards are non-cumulative, non-transferable, non-cashable
    • Allocation is first-come-first-served based on final qualified list
    • Final eligibility is based on end-of-campaign account status

    Important Rules

    • Clients do not need to maintain net deposit during the campaign
    • At the end, both net deposit and trading volume must be met
    • Withdrawals may reset qualification timestamp and affect priority

    Support Required

    • CS Team: Assist client on promotion rules and details and escalate to RC team interested clients.
    • RP Team: Record registered clients during the promotion period.
    • DA Team: Support qualification checks and reward calculation every Monday.
    • VN Marketing: Handle reward delivery.

    Please refer to the attached T&Cs for full details.
