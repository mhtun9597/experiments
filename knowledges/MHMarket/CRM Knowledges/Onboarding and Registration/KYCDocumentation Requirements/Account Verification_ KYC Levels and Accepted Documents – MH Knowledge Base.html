    Account Verification: KYC Levels and Accepted Documents

    Customer Service
    February 05, 2026 12:54 Updated

    Account verification requires clients to submit documents, including Proof of Identity (POI) and Proof of Address (POA). Below are examples of documents that can be uploaded in Sumsub. Based on the region and respective Regulations, we impose 3 KYC Levels. 

    • Examples of Accepted Documents
      • Identity Document (POI)
      • Proof of Address (POA)
      • Selfie
      • EDD Questionnaire
    • Upload Files Restrictions

    Normally, we have to specify the clients based on their region and ID card.

    Basically, if the client's ID card contains proof of address (POA), it is "Level 1" in Sumsub.

    If the client's ID doesn't have proof of address (POA), it will become "Level 2" in Sumsub. 

    If the client registered from the website, the level would be "EDD Check".

    Please refer to the Article ___ for more detailed information on the Level in accordance with Regulation and country of residence. 

    Note:
    For all clients, if there is a potential match with a PEP (Politically Exposed Person), the client must complete the EDD Questionnaire. Their verification level will be updated to "EDD Questionnaire".

    Examples of Accepted Documents

    1. Identity Document (POI)
      • ID Card (National Card)
      • Resident Permit Card
      • Passport
      • Driving License
    2. Proof of Address (POA)
      • Utility Bills - Electricity, water, gas, or landline phone bills (must be issued within the last 3 months)
      • Bank Statements: Recent statements from a bank or credit card company showing the customer's address (usually within the last 3 months)
      • Government-Issued Documents: Voter ID card, or any official correspondence from a government body.
      • Lease or Rental Agreement: A current lease or rental agreement signed by both the landlord and tenant.
      • Property Tax Receipt: A recent receipt for property taxes paid (must be within the last 3 months)
      • Insurance Documents: Home or renters' insurance policy or statement.
      • Residential Certificate: Issued by a municipal corporation or local government entity.
    Note:
    • The Proof of address should be issued within last 3 months.
    • POA document should display the individual's name and address clearly and be recent to ensure they reflect the current residence.
    1. Selfie
      • A clear selfie for identity verification
    2. EDD Questionnaire
      • Questionnaire to gather additional information about the client's financial activities and risk profile.

    Upload Files Restrictions

    • Blurry ID Pictures
    • Graphically edited
    • Screenshots are not accepted
