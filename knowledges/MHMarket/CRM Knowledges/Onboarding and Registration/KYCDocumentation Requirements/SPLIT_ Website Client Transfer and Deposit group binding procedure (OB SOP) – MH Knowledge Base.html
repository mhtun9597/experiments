    SPLIT: Website Client Transfer and Deposit group binding procedure (OB SOP)

    Willow (OB)
    March 03, 2026 10:27 Updated

    For Website Client Manual Openings:

    • We will open accounts BCSD.GB only.

    • We need to bind deposit group; no further action is required from our side (e.g., assigning to the relevant sales according to the website client procedure).

      Note: Marketing team will update the assignment later and notify the Sales team.

     

    Sales/IB Claims for Website Clients (Website Client Claim)

    i. Sales Claims for Website Clients (Website Client Claim)

    Note:

    • Sales can claim the website clients within 24 hours and directly transfer the website clients to the requested sales.
    • If it exceeds 24 hours, we will proceed according to normal referrer changing procedure.

    Step 1: The sales will submit ticket, indicating that their client accidentally registered through the official website and requesting to transfer the website client to under the sales.

     

    Step 2: We must confirm that the sales claimed the client within 24 hours. Once confirmed, the website client can be directly transferred to their name.

    Transfer the client to the designated sales.

    Step 1: First, click on “Users” in the CRM system.

    Step 2: Under the “Users” option, click on “Traders Users.”

    Step 3: After clicking, the following interface will appear. In section 3, choose a method to query the client, such as by client name or email. Then, enter the client information.

    Step 4: Click on "Search".

    Step 5: The corresponding client information will be displayed; then click on the green "+" symbol.

    Step 6: Next, click on the "Edit" symbol.

     

    Step 7: Once in the "Edit" interface, click on "Basic Info."

    Step 8: In the "Supervisor Agent" section, fill in the assigned salesperson.

    Step 9: After confirming that the entered sales is the designated one, click on "Submit." After completing the above steps, please document the changes in the Changing File.

    Step 3: We need to remove 'website client' annotation as shown in the screenshot below.

    • Since only website clients can view all account types (STD, ECN, and Prime), and we need to remove the 'website client' annotation. Otherwise, they will still see all account types.

    Step 4: Once above steps are complete, we can respond to the ticket.

    *Please follow the steps in the following link: Macro Reply.

     

    ii. IB Claims for Website Clients (Website Client Claim)

    Note:

    IB can claim the website clients within 24 hours and directly transfer the website clients to the requested sales.

    If it exceeds 24 hours, we will proceed according to normal referrer changing procedure.

     

    Step 1: The IB submits a ticket stating that their client accidentally registered through the official website and requests to transfer the client under him/her.

    Step 2: Before proceeding with the transfer, we must verify whether the client’s information provided by the IB matches the details of the website client (e.g., email address and name).

    Step 3: If the client information matches, we can proceed with the transfer by recording the changes in the Changing File.

    Step 4: Before transferring, we must ensure that the trading account applied for by the website client has the rebate set up by the IB.

     

    Situation 1: The IB has already set up the applied trading account group
    Step 5: Proceed with the transfer immediately by recording the changes in the Changing File.

    Step 6: We need to remove 'website client' annotation as shown in the screenshot below.

    Since only website clients can view all account types (STD, ECN, and Prime), and we need to remove the 'website client' annotation. Otherwise, they will still see all account types.

     

    Step 7: Once above steps are complete, we can respond to the ticket.

    Please follow the steps in the following link to respond to the ticket: Macro Reply.

     

    Situation 2: The IB has not yet set up the applied trading account group
    Step 5: Send an email to the IB's account manager to confirm the rebate.

    Step 6: Once the account manager provides the rebate template, set up the rebate for the IB.

    How to set rebate for an IB in "Broker Users" (OB SOP)

     

    Step 7: After setting up the rebate, proceed with transferring the client to the IB by recording the changes in the Changing File. And we also need to remove 'website client' annotation

    Step 8: After completing the transfer, we can respond to the ticket.

    Please follow the steps in the following link to respond to the ticket: Macro Reply.

     

    iii.  Sale/IB Claiming Client During the Registration Process

    Step 1 : If Sale/IB mentions that their client accidentally registered through the official website and claims the client while the client is still in the registration process, we need to change the client's level from

    i) "EDD_check" to "Level_1", "China_Level_1", or

    ii) "MENA_Region_EDD_Check_MU" to "MENA_Region_Level_1_MU", or

    iii) "COM_EDD_Check" to "COM_level_1", according to the client’s relevant region.

    This allows the client to skip the face verification, Phone verification and Proof of Address (POA) steps based on the provided ID and region.

    • For example, for "Level_1" and "China_Level_1", if the client's ID includes address, client do not need to provide POA.
    • For COM_level_1, client only need to submit ID.
      However, for "MENA_Region_Level_1_MU", the client may still be required to submit POA.

    Step 2: Once the client completes registration, we need to update the upper IB to the requested Sale/IB. The deposit group will be bound automatically based on the client’s provided information. All other steps will follow the normal account opening procedure.

    Please follow the steps in the following link: Change in Deposit Group (OB SOP)
