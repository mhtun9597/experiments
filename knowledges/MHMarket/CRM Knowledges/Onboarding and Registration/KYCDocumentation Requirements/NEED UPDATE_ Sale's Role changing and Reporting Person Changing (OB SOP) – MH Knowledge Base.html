    NEED UPDATE: Sale's Role changing and Reporting Person Changing (OB SOP)

    KSLIM (OB)
    March 03, 2026 07:50 Updated
    • Situation 1:  If issue sale(AM) is changed to transfer under other TL
    • Situation 2 : Position want to change for issue sale is the same as or higher than new superior
    • Situation 3:TL move under other RGM Tree Line and RGM move under other BD tree

    HR will send a ticket requesting us to transfer the sales to a new reporting person.

     

    Situation 1：

    The issue sale(AM) is changed to other TL, from TL under RGM we can proceed with transferring the sales directly to new reporting person.

    Directly transfer the sales to the new reporter

     

    • We have to change "Main Dept" and "Report To" person. For more detail changing see the below steps.

    Step 1: First, click on "System Management" in the CRM system.

    Step 2: Under the "System Management" option, click on "Dept. and Personnel."

    Step 3: In position 3, enter the sales 's name or email address.

    Step 4: Click "Search."

    Step 5: The corresponding sales will be displayed; click on the green "+" icon

    Step 6: Click the "Edit" icon.

     

    Step 7: Next, in the "Main Dept" field, adjust the sales to the new superior.

    Step i) In position i, enter the new superior's name.

    Step ii) The following interface will appear; select the corresponding sales.

    Step iii) Once selected, the corresponding sales will be displayed in position iii.

     

    Step 8: Next, adjust the sale to the new superior in the "Reporting to" section.

    Step i) Enter the name of the new superior sale in position i.

    Step ii) The following interface will appear; select the corresponding sales.

    Step iii) Once selected, the relevant sales will be displayed in position iii.

    Step 9: After filling everything out, double-check that the information matches what HR provided. Once confirmed, click "Submit."

    Step 10: After submitting, check to see if the addition was successful. If it was added successfully, it will appear as shown in the image below.

     

    Step 11: Once the transfer of the sales to the new superior is complete, we can respond to the ticket.

    Please follow the steps in the following link to respond to the ticket : Macro Reply.

    Step 12: After completing the above steps, please record it in the Changing File.

     

    Situation 2:

    If the position want to change for issue sale is the same as or higher than new superior, we need to confirm with HR whether to lower the sales position or to promote the superior's position.

     

    Once HR has confirmed, we can proceed with transferring the sales to new superior

     

    Downgrade the issue sale position from TL to AM and transfer under other TL

    When changing Team Leader (TL) role to Account Manager (AM), please follow these steps:

    • Check if there are any AMs under the TL.

    1. If no AMs are assigned, we can proceed future process.

    2. If AMs are assigned, we need to confirm new sale to transfer before proceeding with the role change.

    • We have to change "Main Dept", "Position", "Role Name", "Report To" person, "Real name" and "English name" as HR requested. 
    • Need to delete team of sale after making sure that there don't have any other sales under issue sale.

    For more detail changing see the below steps.

     

    Step 1: First, click on "System Management" in the CRM system.

    Step 2: Under the "System Management" option, click on "Dept. and Personnel."

    Step 3: In position 3, enter the sales 's name or email address.

    Step 4: At location 4, we can see the sales team for these sales; click on it.

    Step 5: The corresponding sales, and any sales under the issue sale, will be displayed.

    Step 6: Click on the green "+" icon

    Step 7: Click the "Edit" icon.

     

    Step 8: Based on the sales position provided by HR, modify the sales 's position in the "Position" field. Additionally, make sure to select the appropriate option based on the sales 's work location.

    Step i) In position i, enter the new position to which the sales will be downgraded.

    Step ii) Since the sales is in Dubai (DB), please select "AM (DB)."

    Step iii) Once selected, the sales 's position will be displayed in position iii.

     

    Step 9: Select the appropriate position in the "Role Name" field.

    Step i) Uncheck the original sales 's "TL" position.

    Step ii) After unchecking, select the appropriate position.

     

    Step 10: Select the appropriate position in the "Rebate Tier" field.

    Step i) Click on the sales position you wish to select.

     

    Step ii) After selecting, the sales 's position will be displayed in position ii.

     

    Step 11: Change the position next to the Sale's name (as shown in the image below).

    • If the sales representative was originally a TL (Team Lead), it should be changed to AM (Account Manager).
    • Ensure consistency in both the "Real Name" and "English Name," so these changes should be made together.

    Step 12: Next, adjust the sales to the new supervisor in the "Main Dept."

    Step i) In position i, enter the name of the new supervisor.

    Step ii) The following interface will appear; selected the corresponding sales.

    Step iii) Once selected, the selected sales will be displayed in position iii.

     

    Step 13: Next, adjust the sales's reporting structure to the new supervisor in the "Reporting To" field.

    Step i) In position i, enter the name of the new supervisor.

    Step ii) The following interface will appear; select the corresponding sales.

    Step iii) Once selected, the selected sales will be displayed in position iii.

     

    Step 14: After filling everything out, double-check that the information matches what HR provided. Once confirmed, click "Submit."

    Step 15: After the transfer is successful, check the original sales team of the transferred sales.

    Step 16: If no sales are displayed in the team (as shown in the image below), please delete the team.

    Here is how to delete Team of TL

    Step 17: Check to see if the adjustment was successful. If it was adjusted successfully, it will appear as shown in the image below.

     

    Step 18: Once the transfer of the sales account to the new superior is complete, we can respond to the ticket.

    Please follow the steps in the following link to respond to the ticket : Macro Reply.

    Step 19: After completing the above steps, please document the changes in the Changing File and Auditing.

    • Need to record in Changing File for role changing and Reported person changing.

     

     

    Situation 3:

    TL move under other RGM Tree Line and RGM move under other BD tree

    When changing Team Leader (TL) to under other RGM tree line, please follow these steps:

    • We have to change "Superior Dept" of team to the RGM that we want to move for.
    • We have to change reported person also in issue sale's CRM.

    Step 1 : Go to "Dept. and Personnel" tab of "System Management".

    Step 2 : Type the issue sale's team name and relevant sale's team will appear.

    Step 3 : Click on the edit button according to the below screenshot.

    Step 4 : We have to change "Superior Dept." to the BD that we want to move for and click on "Submit".

     

    Step 5 : We need to change reported person. Fine the sale's CRM with sale's mail or name. And click on edit button as shown on screenshot.

     

    Step 6 : Have to change reported person to relevant BD and click on submit.

     

     

    Note -RGM move under other BD  tree is also same process with TL move under other RGM.

    • We have to change "Superior Dept" of team to the BD that we want to move for.
    • We have to change reported person also in issue sale's CRM.
