    Procedure for Client Becoming a PAMM Money Manager

    CS Assistant
    February 03, 2026 14:00 Updated

    This article outlines the procedure for a client to become a PAMM Money Manager, including requirements, necessary steps, and account restrictions.

    Scope

    This procedure applies to:

    • Money Managers
    • Investors
    • Fee Accounts

    PAMM Application workflow

    Step 1: Application Submission

    • Sales submits PAMM application request. 
    • CS verifies:
      • Application form completeness. It must be completed fully and included all necessary information.
      • PAMM Service Agreement is signed
    • If requirements are met, CS forwards request to Onboarding (OB) Team.

    Step 2: PAMM Requirements

    Before continuing with the PAMM application, OB should perform the following checkpoint:

    * If you find that the client’s requested group is a markup group and it’s not in the ready list, please contact the OP team to arrange for group creation prior to proceeding.

    1. Performance Fee Ratio : It is a fee for that will be calculated and charged into Fee account based on Money Manager trading performance. Shown as % .

    2. Management Fee : It is a fee for connecting to Money Manager. It will be charged from Investor accounts into Fee accounts as soon as PAMM connected. Shown as %

    3. ** More information about PAMM Manager Fees can be found here: PAMM Manager Fees Explained – MH Markets

    How to check if Account is Connected to Social Trading? 

    Social Trading Access for Check:

    1. Dashboard Link: Home | Social Trading

    2. Login: MHM-CS-01

    3. Password: f'cC[1{.o4*5

    Step 3: Change account group to PAMM group: 

    • Change Money Manager and Fee account into " MT4 > #_STD_P##SM-USD | MT5 > #_STD_P##S_MAMM " based on its original group. Moreover, we should also close deposit and withdraw functions and change rebate method into " COM0 ", but not Fee account.
    • Change investor account into " MT4 > C_STD_P##SS-USD | MT5 > C_STD_P00S_MAMS " based on its original group and close their trading access.

    Step 4: PAMM Connection

    If all requirements are met, the Ob team can escalate to the OP to the PAMM service activation. OP connects PAMM account and opens client portals in PAMM system. client will receive credentials to login to PAMM Portal.

    Additional Notes

    • PAMM accounts are not eligible for promotions.
    • If Money Manager, Fee, or Investor accounts are in A group, need to inform the RC team for further confirmation.
    • If investor equity falls below zero, PAMM will auto-disconnect.
      • Client receives email notification.
      • Reconnection requires resubmission of the form.
    • If requested account was previously used for PAMM:
      • Inform client to open a new account.
      • If client insists on reusing, notify OP for confirmation.
