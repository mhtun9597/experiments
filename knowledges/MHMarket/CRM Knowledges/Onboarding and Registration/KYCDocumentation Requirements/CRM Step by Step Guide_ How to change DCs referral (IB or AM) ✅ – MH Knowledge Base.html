    CRM Step by Step Guide: How to change DCs referral (IB or AM) ✅

    Willow (OB)
    February 05, 2026 12:15 Updated

    This is a step-by-step guide on how to move DC under the new IB or AM. The process consists of 4 steps:

    1. Verify the client is DC
    2. Check the trading account groups that the client has opened
    3. Check rebate for DC client account type in new IB
    4. DC moves under Sale

    Process Flowchart

    Each step outlined in great details below: 

    Step 1: Verify the client is DC

    1. In the CRM system click on “Users” > “Traders Users.”
    2. Enter client's email and click on "Search."
    3. We can copy the client's name or identification code. 
    4. Check the trading accounts groups for that client.

    Step 2: Check client's trading accounts groups

    1. In the CRM system click on "Users" > "MT Account".
    2. Search for the client and check all the different trading accounts types they have opened. 

    Step 3: Check if New IB has necessary rebate schemes

    Follow this guide to check rebate schemes: How to check IB's Rebate (OB SOP).

       3.1: Rebate scheme ALREADY exists

    If the new IB has rebate schemes for all account types the client has, then you can proceed with the change to the Step 4.

       3.2: Rebate scheme does NOT exist

    If the new IB does not have the rebate schemes for one of the accounts, please proceed as follows:

    1. Confirm the rebate scheme for all account types of a transfer client with the new IB's SalesPerson.
    2. Once the IB replies to the ticket and provides the rebate templates > set up the rebate for the new IB.
      Please follow the steps in the following article: How to set rebate for an IB in "Broker Users" (OB SOP)
    3. Once the Rebates are set up, you can proceed with moving the client under the new referral >  Step 4.

       3.3: Client moves under AM

    If DC is moving under the AM (Salesperson) - there is no need to check the rebate for the account type, as the commission for Sales is calculated monthly by the DA team. And we directly proceed to move DC client under the AM > to the Step 4.

    Step 4: Make a record

    Before processing with the change > make a record in the Changing File > Referrer, Rebate, Trade Group tab. 

    Step 5: Proceed with the Request

    1. In the CRM system click on “Users” > “Traders Users.”
    2. In section 3, choose a method to query the client, such as by client name or email. Then, enter the client information >  Click on "Search".
    3. The corresponding client information will be displayed; then click on the green "+" symbol.
    4. Next, click on the "Edit" symbol.
    5. Once in the "Edit" interface, click on "Basic Info."
    6. In the "Supervisor Agent" section, fill in the assigned salesperson.
    7. After confirming that the entered sales is the designated one, click on "Submit."

    Step 6: Reply to the Ticket

    Once the client has been successfully transferred to the new sale, and the changes have been recorded in the Changing File, we may proceed to respond to the ticket.

    Please follow the steps in the following link to respond to the ticket: Macro Reply
