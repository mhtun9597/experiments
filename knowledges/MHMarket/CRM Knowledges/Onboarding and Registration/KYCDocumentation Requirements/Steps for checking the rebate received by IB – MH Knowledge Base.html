    Steps for checking the rebate received by IB

    KSLIM (OB)
    March 03, 2026 10:38 Updated

    Sales team will request the rebate checking

     

    Step 1: First, we need to verify the subtype of the trading account. Please follow the steps outlined below.

    Step 1：Click on the "User" in the CRM system.

    Step 2：Under "User" click on "MT Account".

    Step 3：After clicking, the following interface will appear. Select the method to query the client, such as client name / identification code / email address. After that, fill in the client information.

    Step 4： Once completed, click on "Search."

    Step 5：The corresponding client information will be displayed.

    Step 6：Click on "Switch View."

    *The following interface shows different presentation methods. The initial query interface only 
displays the trading accounts, and does not allow editing or viewing of the 
IB's rebate template. You need to switch to another interface to edit and view.

    Step 7: The corresponding IB information will be displayed. After confirming it’s correct, click the teal “+” icon.

    Step 8: Then we can found the out the subtype of the trading account in "Spread Rebate Group"

     

    Step 2: Next, we need to verify that the IB has set up the rebate for the specific subtype. Please follow the steps outlined below.

    *Please refer the following link Method for Checking the IB's Rebate (OB SOP) for instructions on checking the rebate.

    Step 3: Once it is confirmed that the IB has set up the rebate for the specific subtype, we need to check whether the IB has been set according to the sales requirements.

    Way 1: We can review the case in Zendesk using the IB's email or name to verify if the rebate we set up matches the rebate template provided by the sales team.

    Way 2: We can check the specific file: Changing File to see if the Sales requested an adjustment to the rebate for the specific subtype. If so, we need to verify in Zendesk whether the rebate we set up matches the rebate template provided by Sales.

     

    Situation 1: If we notice that we have set incorrect rebate for the IB

    Step 1: First, record the incorrect rebate in the Changing File

    Step 2: Then, update the correct rebate for the IB based on the latest rebate template provided by the sales team.

    Step 3: Escalate the ticket to RP Team for assistance with the rebate recalculation.

     

    Step 4: After that, we need to confirm that the trading account has closed all the positions, as the rebate is calculated and paid to the IB when the trading account position is closed. You can check the status of the client's trading account in MT4/MT5 Manager. Additionally, you can also review the trading history directly from the MT Manager.

     

    Situation 2: If rebate set up is correct and don't have any changing .

    Step 1:  We need to request  Trade Account ID, Order IDs, and the date range (period) for the relevant orders. Once we received the necessary details, we can proceed for future step of checking commission.

    Step 2: We Need check client's trading account status and trading history in MT4&MT5 platform.

    Please refer to the following link for the MT4/MT5 Manager.

    Step 3: We need to view the trading history in the CRM. Check whether the transactions match with the manager and CRM.

    Step 1: Next, click on "Report" in the CRM system.

    Step 2: Under "User," click on "Trading Orders".

    Step 3: Select "Trading History" at position 3.

    Step 4: Select "Account" at position 4.

    Step 5: Enter the trading account you want to search for.

    Step 6: Select "all_lists" at position 6.

    Step 7：Select "Close Time" at position 7.

    Step 8：Please adjust the time period you want to search for.

    Step 9: Press "Search"

    Step 10: Detailed information will be displayed, including the products the client traded, the lot sizes, and other relevant details.

     

    • It is possible that some orders did not reach the CRM from MT. Therefore, we need to inform the CRM Developer team under the 'Current CRM Issue Solving' group to sync all transactions to the CRM.

     

    Step 4: If everything is in order, we need to verify whether the rebate has been paid to the IB.

    Step 1: Next, click on "Report" in the CRM system.

    Step 2: Under "Report", click on "Rebate Commissions".

    Step 3: Depending on the trading account you want to check, select the corresponding MT server.

     

    Step 4: Select "all lists" at position 4.

     

    Step 5：Select "Close Time" at position 5.

     

    Step 6：Please adjust the time period you want to search for.

    Step 7: Select "Account Number" at position 7.

     

    Step 8：Enter the trading account you wish to check

    Step 9: Click on "Search"

    Step 10: Then, click the green "+" icon.

    Step 11: The related commission rebate transaction history will be displayed here, including detailed information such as the products traded, lot sizes, and other relevant details.

    Step 12: Here will show the total lots, rebate and total rebate

    *We need to confirm the rebate is it correct

    Total lots x Rebate of product traded= Total Rebate
