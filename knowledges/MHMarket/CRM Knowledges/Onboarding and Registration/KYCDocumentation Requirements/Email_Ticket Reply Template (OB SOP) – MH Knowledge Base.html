    Client POA not approved

    Scenario Replied to the ticket
    Client POA not approved

    Customers are required to provide a valid Proof of Address Document (POA).
    Please assist the customer to provide any of the following valid POA documents, and the document date must not be older than 3 months:

    - Utility bills,
    - Bank or credit card statements,
    - Tax invoice or government-issued residential statement/certificate

     

    客户必须提供有效的地址证明。 文件必须包括以下任何一项，不早于 3 个月：
    - 水电费账单，
    - 银行/信用卡对帐单，
    - 税务发票或政府签发的住宅声明/证明。

    我们不接受：
    - 手机账单，
    - 医疗费，
    - 收据或保险单。
