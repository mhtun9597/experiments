    Opening a Demo and Real Demo Accounts: Overview and SOP

    CS Assistant
    March 23, 2026 08:07 Updated

    The difference between Demo and Real demo accounts is that the latter is created on the Live servers with the real sever conditions. Due to its nature, the Live demo accounts opening required a BD approval.

    • Demo account
      • Open Demo Account SOP
    • Real Demo account
      • Open Real Demo Account opening SOP
      • Real Demo account available markup groups

    Demo Account Opening Process Flowchart

    Demo account 

    • Each client can open up to 10 MT4 and 1 MT5 demo account.

    • Only Standard (STD), ECN, and Prime account types are available for demo account opening. 
    • There are no markup groups for Demo accounts.

    • The maximum initial deposit or top-up is USD 100,000 (for both MT4 and MT5).

    • Demo accounts expire after 6 months. MT5 demo accounts cannot be extended. Only MT4 demo accounts are eligible for extension by request to the Operations team. MT4 demo accounts are permanently deleted 365 days after creation.

    • Fixed Leverage available only. and can be set up to 1:2000, based on the client’s preference.

    • The name, email address, and phone number on demo accounts cannot be modified.
    • OB is responsible for opening new demo accounts. For any changes, requests must be forwarded to the Operations team in JIRA.

    Demo Account Opening SOP

    🔹Step 1: Select Theme "Open Demo account"
    🔹Step 2: Guide client to open account in Dashboard.

    When clients request a demo account, CSR should first guide them to open it via the client portal or the website first. Here are the instructions on how to How to Create a Demo Trading Account with MH Markets within Client's Dashboard.

    🔹Step 3: Collect Necessary Information

    If the client is unable to do so, we may open the demo account manually. First CSR confirm the following information:

    • Client Name
    • Phone Number
    • Client's Email
    • Account Group (Standard, ECN, or Prime)
    • Platform (MT4 or MT5)
    • Leverage
    • Deposit Amount
    🔹Step 4: Forward request to OB

    Once all necessary information is collected, CSR forward request to OB team. 

    🔹Step 5: OB to create Demo account

    OB team will create a new Demo account for a client following the CRM steps described the CRM Step by Step Guide: Open Demo Account article. Upon Completion OB notifies the CS

    Step 6: CSR to notify the client

    Real Demo account

    Real Demo accounts are only allowed for business development purposes, with prior approval from John. Only RGM and BD are authorized to submit live demo account opening requests. Requests from AMs and TLs will not be accepted.

    What is the Real Demo Accounts limits per region?

    If the approval limit for Tim is out of his range, please let Tim reach out to Justin for more approval.

    • The maximum balance limit for a live demo account is USD 100,000.

    • For balances within the limit, BD/RGM (or their assistant) approval is sufficient.

    • For balances exceeding the limit, a clear and valid reason (e.g., marketing activity, advertising campaign) must be provided, and approval must be obtained from both BD/RGM and RC.

    • The standard validity period for real demo accounts is one month. This applies globally for all clients. Extensions are allowed upon approval. Each extension grants an additional one month of usage. The number of extensions is subject to approval based on individual cases. 

    Open Real Demo Account Opening SOP

    1. Only requests from Sales are accepted (clients cannot request Real Demo accounts). Sales should provide the reason for the Real Demo account request.
    2. CSR sends a side conversation to get approval from the RGM.
    3. If Approval received, CSR to collect necessary information: 
      • Client Name
      • Phone Number
      • Client's Email
      • Account Group (Standard, ECN, or Prime)
      • Platform (MT4 or MT5)
      • Leverage
      • Deposit Amount
    4. Once all necessary information is collected, CSR forward request to OP team. 
    5. Upon Completion OP notifies the CS
    6. CSR to notify the client and Solve the ticket.

    Once all necessary information is collected, you can follow the CRM steps to open a Demo account. Steps are described in detail in the Opening a Demo and Live Demo Accounts: CRM Step by Step Guide article.

    Real Demo Available Markup Groups

    Eligible account types: Standard (STD), ECN, and Prime only.
