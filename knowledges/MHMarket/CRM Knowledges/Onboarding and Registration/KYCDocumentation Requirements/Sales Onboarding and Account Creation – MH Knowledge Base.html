    Create Sales Account

    1. HR must submit a ticket requesting the creation of sales account for any new sales team member.
      The ticket should include all necessary details such as the Real name, CRM display name, Position, Joining Date, Work location, Reporting line and CRM mail.
    2. OB will open the sales account based on the sales information provided by the HR department in the ticket.

    CRM Step by Step Guide on Creating the AM's CRM account 

    Step 1: First, click on "System Management" in the CRM system.

    Step 2: Under the "System Management" option, click on "Dept. and Personnel."

    Step 3: Once the following interface is displayed, click "Add."

    Step 4: Once you enter the following interface, we need to fill in the corresponding fields based on the sales information provided by the HR department (as shown in the screenshot below).

    Step 5: In the "Login Account" and "Mailbox" fields, enter the sales 's email address.

    Step 6: The "Login Password" and "Confirm Password" must match. We can create a random password using the Password Generator website. After creating and filling it in, make sure to record it, as it needs to be sent to the sales for them to log in and change the password themselves.

    Step 7: In the "Main Dept." field, enter the name of the sales 's superior (the superior listed in "Reporting Manager" by HR).

    Step i) In (i), enter the name of the superior.

    Step ii) The following interface will appear; we will select the corresponding sales.

    Step iii) Once selected, the corresponding sales will be displayed in position iii.

     

    Step 8: Based on the sales position provided by HR, fill in the relevant information in the "Position" field. Additionally, we need to select according to the sales's work location.

    Step i) Enter the sales position in position (i).

    Step ii) Since the sales is in Vietnam, we select "AM (VN)."

    Step iii) Once selected, the sales position will be displayed in Position (iii).

     

    Step 9: In the "Role Name" field, select the corresponding position.

    Step i) Click on the sales position you want to select.

    Step ii) After selecting, the sales position will be displayed in position ii.

     

    Step 10: In the "Rebate Tier" field, select the corresponding position.

    Step i) Click on the sales position you want to select.

    Step ii) After selecting, the sales position will be displayed in position ii.

     

    Step 11: In the "Report to" field, select the sales 's superior.

     

    Step 12: The "Real Name" and "English Name" must match. We will fill in the name based on the "Display Name." that provided by HR. After entering the name, we need to add the sales representative's position after their name.

    For example, if the sales is an AM (Account Manager), we will append "AM" to name, as shown in the image below.

     

    Step 13: Enter the "Entry Time" using the information provided by HR.

     

    Step 14: After filling everything out, double-check that the information matches what HR provided. Once confirmed, click "Submit."

    Step 15: After submitting, check to see if the addition was successful. If it was added successfully, it will appear as shown in the image below.

     

    Step 16: Once the sales account is successfully created, we can respond to the ticket.

    Creating the TL's CRM and Team

    When creating a TL sales account, the steps are the same as for a regular sales account, except that we need to add the corresponding sales team in the main department.

    Process to create a new sales team

    Step 1: First, we need to identify the reporting manager. HR will inform us who it is.

    Step 2: Next, we need to create the new sales team in the CRM.

    Process to create a new sales team

    Step 1: First, click on "System Management" in the CRM system.

    Step 2: Under the "System Management" option, click on "Dept. and Personnel."

    Step 3: Enter the reporting manager's name in position 3.

    Step 4: There will show MayaRGM in position 4, then click the icon "Add".

    Step 5: After that, an interface like the one above will pop up. Then, we need to enter the sales team name in the "Dept. Name" field.

    Step 6: Then, click "Submit."

    Step 7: After creating the new sales team, assign the new TL to the team when setting up the TL sales account.

    Step i) In position (i), enter the name of the sales team.

    Step ii) The following interface will appear; we will select the corresponding sales team.

    Step iii) Once selected, the corresponding sales team will be displayed in position iii.

    Step 8: After creating the TL sales account, we need to make some updates to the new sales team.

    Step 9: Enter the sales team's name in position 9.

    Step 10: The sales team will appear in position 10. Then, click the "Edit" icon.

     

    Step 11: Enter the TL sales account name in the 'Dept. Head' field.

    Step 12: Enter the TL sales account name in the 'Dept. Observer' field.

    Step 13: Then, click "Submit."

    Creating RGM's CRM and Team

    When creating an RGM account, follow the same steps as for TL account.

    • Create Sale Team and CRM specifically for the RGM.

    Use the detailed information provided by the HR team when filling out the required fields.

    All other procedures remain the same as TL sales account creation.

    Note - If HR requests to create AM accounts under an RGM, we must first create a Sales Team under the RGM (as shown in the reference image).
    We cannot create AM accounts directly under RGM without designated team structure in place.
