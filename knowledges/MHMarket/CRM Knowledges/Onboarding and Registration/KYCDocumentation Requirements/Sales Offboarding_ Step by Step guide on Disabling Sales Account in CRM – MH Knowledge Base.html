    Sales Offboarding: Step by Step guide on Disabling Sales Account in CRM

    Willow (OB)
    January 13, 2026 12:49 Updated

    The HR Department usually sends a ticket requesting us to de-activate the accounts of resigned sales and transfer their IBs & clients to the new sales users. The Offboarding procedure and last commission payout workflow is outlined here: Sales Offboarding: Account Termination and Final Commission Payout Procedures.

    In this article we will review the step-by-step process on disabling the Sales CRM account. On the high-level it consists of the following steps:

    1. Check if there are any IBs under resigned salesperson
    2. Confirm DCs under resigned salesperson
    3. Identify possible situations
      • Situation 1: There are Clients (DCs) under the resigned sale
      • Situation 2: There don't have clients (DC) under resigned sale
    4. Transfer and disable account
    5. Record in Changing File
    6. Resignation for TL and RGM

    Step 1: Check if there are any IBs under resigned salesperson

    Before disabling the resigned Sales' account, we need to check if there are any client under resigned sale or not. If there are clients to be transferred, we should follow the stages below.

    1. First, click on “Users” in the CRM system.
    2.  Under the “Users” option, click on “Broker Users.”
    3. Enter the email or name of the departing sales in position 3.
    4. The system will display the relevant sales; click on the corresponding departing sales.
    5. In the "Tier" field, select MIB (Master IB).
    6. Click "Search.
    7. The MIB under the departing sales will be displayed.
    8. Tick all MIB in the box at location 8. (We are transferring the MIB because the sub IB and clients under the MIB will also be transferred to the new sales.)
    9.  Click “Bulk Operation” 
    10. In the "Bulk Operations" section, click on "Batch Transfer."
    11. Once the pop-up interface appears, change the sales under the resigned to the new sales.
    12. Click "Submit."
    13.  In the following interface, click "Neglect." (Note: "Delete All" will remove all rebate for the transferred IBs, so please avoid clicking it randomly.)
    14. Next, click "Continue to Transfer."

    Step 2: Confirm DCs under resigned salesperson

    Check whether the resigned salesperson still has any DCs assigned.

    1. First, click on "User" in the CRM system.
    2. Under "User" click on "Trader Users" and check on "Broker User" as well.
    3. In number 3 below, enter the email or name of the resigned sales.
      After checking, there may have two situations:
    4. The system will display the relevant sales; click on the corresponding resigned sales representative.
    5. The clients under the resigned sales will appear. 

    Step 3: Identify possible situations

    After checking, determine which of the two situations applies (e.g., clients/DCs present or not).

       Situation 1: There are Clients (DCs) under the resigned sale

    so we need to transfer the DCs to new sale. Transfer the DCs under the resigned sales to new sale.

    1. First, click on "User" in the CRM system.
    2. Under "User," click on "Trader User."
    3. In field 3, enter the email or name of the departed sales representative.
    4. The system will display the relevant sales; click on the corresponding resigned sales.
    5. Choose "Directly Affiliated"
    6. The clients under the departed sales will be displayed.
    7. In the box at field 8, check all clients.
    8. Click on "Bulk Operations."
    9. After entering the following interface, select "AM" in the "Superior Agent" section.
    10. Select the new sales.
    11. Once confirmed, click "Submit."

       Situation 2: There don't have clients (DC) under resigned sale

    In that case OB can proceed with disabling the sales account.

    Step 4: Transfer and disable account

    Ensure all IBs and clients have been successfully transferred to the new salesperson. Then, disable the resigned salesperson’s account.

    After verifying the successful transfer of all IBs and clients to the new sales, we will proceed to disable the account of the resigning sales.

    Disable the account of resign sales

    1. First, click on "System Management" in the CRM system.
    2. Under the "System Management" option, click on "Dept. and Personnel."
    3. In position 3, enter the sales 's name or email address.
    4. Click "Search."
    5. The corresponding sales will be displayed
    6. Click the "Edit" icon.
    7. After the above interface appears, adjust the sales to the new supervisor in the "Main Dept."
      •  Step i) In position (i) > enter "resign."
      • Step ii) The following interface will appear, select the appropriate option based on the sale’s region. (Since this is for CN sales, we select 中国CN (resigned).
      • Step iii) Once checked, the selected option will be displayed in position (iii).
    8. Next, we need to select "Disabled" in the "Status" field.
    9. After filling all the details, double-check that all information is correct. After confirming there are no errors, click "Submit."
    10. We need to verify that the sales has been successfully disabled. (as shown in the image below)
      • The circle under "Login Account" will be yellow.
      • The "Main Dept. Name" must include the word "resigned."

    Step 5: Respond to the ticket

    Use the provided : Macro Reply link to respond to the ticket once the account is disabled.

    Step 6: Record in Changing File

    Log the completed changes in the Changing File.

    Resignation for TL and RGM

    Before disable RGM account, the previous checking and transferring steps are the same as above.

    1. If the resigning staff is TL:

      • Check if there are any AMs and clients (DCs, IBs) under the TL.

      • Reassign AMs and clients as HR provided.

      • Need to delete TL team creation
      • Disable TL's CRM and change main dept to "Resigned"
      • Need to record in changing file.

    1. If the resigning staff is RGM:

      • Check for any TLs, AMs, and clients (DCs, IBs) under the RGM.

      • Reassign all roles and clients as HR provided.

      • Need to delete RGM team creation same as TL Team creation.
      • Disable RGM's CRM and change main dept to "Resigned".
      • Need to record in changing file.
