    CRM Step by Step Guide: Create Business Director (BD) CRM Account

    KSLIM (OB)
    January 20, 2026 07:49 Updated

    In this article, you’ll find both the Create a New CRM Account for Business Director (BD) SOP and the detailed CRM step‑by‑step guide in one place.

    Process Flowchart

    1. HR will send a ticket requesting us to create a Business Director account in CRM for the new staff member. HR team should include necessary information:
      • New BD Name as well as Display name
      • Designation (position)
      • Joining date
      • Work Location
      • Reporting Manager
      • Email address.
    2. OB will proceed with creating a CRM account based on the Business Director's information provided by HR in the ticket.

    3. First, we need to verify in the "Work Location" section of the new BD information provided by HR whether the region he/she is responsible for has already been set up in the CRM system.

    4. If the new BD is assigned to the region that has not yet been set up in the CRM system, we will need to add the region to the CRM > Process for adding a new region under "全球CEO" section
    Important Note:
    Note: If we create a new region under "全球CEO," we must inform Shirley in the "Current CRM Issue Solving" teams' group.
    1. Under the new created region, set up the team for the new BD.
    2. Once the BD team is created, proceed to create the BD CRM account for the new BD.
    3. Once the BD's CRM account is created, we need to go back to the newly created team to add this BD as the "Dept Head" and "Dept Observer." 
    4. Once the BD account and teams is successfully created, we can respond to the ticket.
      Please follow the steps in the following link to respond to the ticket: Macro Reply.
    5. After completing the above steps, please record it in the Changing File and Auditing

    Process for adding a new region under "全球CEO" section

    1. To Set up a new region, first, click on "System Management" in the CRM system.
    2. Under the "System Management" option, click on "Dept. and Personnel."
    3. Next, click "Add" in the "全球CEO" section of the interface.
    4. After that, the interface above will pop up. Next, enter the region name in the "Dept. Name" field.
    5. Once the region name is entered, click on "Submit."
    6. After adding the new region under the "全球CEO" section, refresh the page and double-check to confirm that it has been successfully added to the CRM.
    7. Under the new created region, set up the team for the new BD.

    Process for Creating a new team for new BD CRM 

    1. Click "Add" in the newly created region.
    2. Following this, an interface like the one above will pop up. We need to enter the display name of the BD in the "Dept. Name" field.
      Example: If this team is created for TimBD under the Singapore region, it will be named "TimBD.SG."
    3. Once the display name of the BD is entered, click on "Submit."
    4. Once the BD team is created, proceed to create the BD CRM account for the new BD.

    Process for Creating a BD CRM Account

    1. First, click on "System Management" in the CRM system.
    2. Under the "System Management" option, click on "Dept. and Personnel."
    3. Once the following interface is displayed, click "Add."
    4. Once you enter the following interface, we need to fill in the corresponding field based on the sales information provided by the HR department (as shown in the screenshot below).
    5. In the "Login Account" and "Mailbox" fields, enter the sales 's email address.
    6. The "Login Password" and "Confirm Password" must match. We can create a random password using the Password Generator website. After creating and filling it in, make sure to record it, as it needs to be sent to the sales for them to log in and change the password themselves.
    7. In the "Main Dept." field, enter the name of the sales 's superior (the superior listed in "Reporting Manager" by HR).

    Step i) In position i, enter the name of the team that we just created earlier.

    Step ii) The following interface will appear; we will select the corresponding team.

    Step iii) Once selected, the corresponding team will be displayed in position iii.

    1. Based on the sales position provided by HR, fill in the relevant information in the "Position" field. Additionally, we need to select according to the sales 's work location.
      i) Enter the sales position in position i.
      ii) Since there are no BDs under SG, we have selected "BD (DB).
      iii) Once selected, the sales position will be displayed in Position iii.
    2. In the "Role Name" field, select the corresponding position.
    3. In the "Rebate Tier" field, select the corresponding tier.
    4. In the "Report to" field, select the sales' superior as provided by HR.
    5. The "Real Name" and "English Name" must match. We will fill in the name based on the "Display Name." that provided by HR. After entering the name, we need to add the sales representative's position after their name.
      For example, if the sales is BD (Board Director), we will append "BD" to their name, as shown in the image below.
    6. Insert the "Entry Time" using the information provided by HR.
    7. After filling all the details, double-check that the information matches what HR provided. Once confirmed, click "Submit."
    8. After submitting, check if the addition was successful. If it was added successfully, it will appear as shown in the image below.

    Process to Add a New BD to the Newly Created Team

    1. Click on "Edit" icon near to the TimBD
    2. The following interface will appear. Next, add the BD as the "Dept Head."
    Note:
    • If we create the additional tree lines for sales, we also need to set up the sales' original account as a department observer.
    • Also, Sales can use their original account to view all regions without needing multiple accounts.
    1. Add the BD as the "Dept Observer."

    Step 4: After reviewing and confirming there are no issues, click "Submit."
