    Process of Changing Account Manager

    Willow (OB)
    March 16, 2026 06:22 Updated

    Requests to change account manager are accepted ONLY if it was sent from the client's registered email to support@mhmarkets.com. If a client sends request through another channel (e.g., messaging, website, etc.), we will ask the client to resend the request using their registered email.

    1. If a client wishes to change their assigned IB or Account Manager, they must send the request from their registered email to our respective support email addresses. 

      Requests initiated by Sales below the BD level will not be accepted unless prior BD approval is obtained. Once BD approval is granted, we may proceed with the change accordingly.
    2. Based on the relationship change scope upon receiving a client’s email request the Onboarding team should notify respective owner based on the Type of change matrix below. From this moment, the 48-hour response window starts. 

    Process Flowchart

    Region Specific escalation process:

    Relationship change scope

    **For BD-level referral changes (e.g., from JustinBD to ChokriBD): We will only accept the request if it is submitted directly by the client. The standard 48-hour SOP window still applies. If the client insists, we will proceed after the window period. If the client does not insist, the sales team should assist the client in canceling the request.
    1. If the current account owner agrees, the OB may proceed immediately (no need to wait for 48 hours). 
    Note – Once we receive confirmation from the relevant current sale regarding the requested change, for rebate or future confirmations, we will coordinate with new sale assigned as the client’s requested referrer.
    1. If the current account owner disagrees, the client will be contacted after the 48-hour window using the following template:

    Hello {name},

    Since your account manager has been contacted to you and assisted for your request, we would like to confirm whether you would like to continue the process. Thank you for your confirmation

    • If the client replies “Yes” or insists on proceeding, inform them that the standard processing time is 48 working hours, and they must wait until completion. (For special cases, a special process applies.) > the change will proceed.
    • If the client replies “No” or provides no response > the process will be stopped.
    1. Once the change has been processed, OB must inform the current AM and cc respective owner as per table above that the client has been shifted.
    2. After that OB should confirm and apply the new Rebate scheme. 
    Please note that in any circumstances, the client’s decision takes priority over the sales team decision. 

    Sales-Initiated Requests:

    • As per BD's confirmation, if client's sale send request on behalf of their client, let the requester sale to guide the client to send email to us first. If the sale keeps pushing, we can advise them to contact their BD for approval.

    • If a new sales member requests a client transfer on behalf of the client, we must reject the request. Please advise the sale to ask the client to send email directly to us from registered email address to proceed.

    Example of Changing Account Manager Procedure

    1. The client submits a ticket requesting the transfer to the new sales.

    2. CSR needs to send an email to relevant sales to inform them of the transfer. Therefore, first, check the original sale tree line of the client and new tree line of the referrer. 
    3. After checking original sales team and the client's supervisor requesting the transfer, CSR needs to send an email to the current sale tree line according to client's region and sale's tree line level changing based on Relationship change scope.
