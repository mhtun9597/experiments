    Register and Configure Accounts in Social Trading

    MHMarket Assistant
    September 11, 2025 08:35 Updated

    Social Trading

    All about "Social Trading" 

    • Who is Provider?
    • Register as a Provider
    • Configure a Provider Account
    • Who is Follower?

    • Configure as a Follower Account

    Who is the provider? 

    Provider Account

    Provider account is a trading account that acts as a source for trading orders copied to the Follower's account, including the following functions:

    • Register as a new Provider via Social Trading.
    • Attract Followers: Achieve high rankings on the leaderboard or suggest attractive offers to Followers.
    • Manage settings: Configure and manage Provider-specific settings.
    • Trade: Execute trading orders via Meta Trader as usual.
    • Profit sharing: Earn from Followers based on profitable closing orders.

    Register as the Provider

    Provider Registration Link: https://socialtrading.mhmarkets.org/portal/registration/provider

    • Select "Register as a Provider" and fill in your registration information, then select "Register"
    • Note: The registration password must be your MT 4/ MT 5 login password

     

    Configure Provider Account

    Login as the Provider

    Login Link: https://socialtrading.mhmarkets.org/portal/login

    • Fill in registration information, then select "Login"
    • Note: The login password must also be login password of the MT 4 / MT5

     

    Configure Provider Account

    • Proceed to change information such as Nickname, Settings, Account Configuration

     

    Update Avatar

    • On the settings page, click “Action” and then select “Update Avatar”

    Common Information

    • Change Nickname
    • Public
       - The strategy will be listed on the ranking page; anyone can copy it.
    • Private
      - The strategy is only visible to Followers via Provider's private link.

     

    Personal Detail of Account

    • Summary:
       - A brief summary of the trading strategy
    • Notes:
       - Notes for Followers

     

    Hide Stops

    Allows whether the Stop Loss (SL) and Take Profit (TP) levels of Provider orders are synchronized with the Follower.

    • Hide: SL/TP values will not be synchronized with the Follower's account.
    • Share: SL/TP values will be synchronized with the Follower's account.

    Strategy Mode

    • All: Both open and closed trades are copied to the Follower's account.
    • Out Only: Only currently open orders will be copied and closed in the Follower's account.
      New orders on the Provider's account are not copied to the Follower's account.

    Fee Receiver Account

    • Check the information of the account that receives profits from Social Trading- “Fee Receiver Account”.
    • Default setting: profit sharing is sent to the Provider's trading account.

     

    Offer From Provider

    • Performance Fee: Profit sharing rate for Provider.
    • Interval: Frequency of profit-sharing payment for Provider.
    • Visibility: Public or Private (view via private link for Follower).

    • Provider must have at least 1 Offer for Follower.
    • Provider can create multiple Offers to attract Followers.
    • Provider can create Offers with different minimum balances. If Follower has high account balance, performance fee should be low.

    • Private Offers are only available to Followers who join via private links.
    • It is not possible to modify profit sharing settings or payment times while a follower is active.
    • To make changes, the Provider must archive the strategy (disconnecting all Followers) or wait for the Follower to unsubscribe.

    • Providers can create new Offers at any time, can change the visibility of existing Offers to "Private" and remove the join link if they want to prevent new Followers from signing up for the current Offer.

    Archive the Strategy

    • This action will close all open orders of the Follower.
    • Active orders of the Provider will remain running until manually closed by the Provider.

    WHO IS FOLLOWER

    Follower Account

    A follower account is a customer's trading account that copies orders from a Provider's account. It includes the following functionalities:

    • Register to copy trades from multiple Providers.
    • Search to copy Providers through the Ranking page or via a private link.
    • Manage copied orders.
    • Receive profits from orders closed by the Provider.
    • Share profits with Providers according to the selected Offer.

    Register as the Follower

    Follower registration

    • Select "Register as a Follower" and fill in your registration information, then select "Register"
    • Note: The registration password must be your login password of MT 4 / MT 5

    Configure Follower Account

    Login as a Follower

    Follower Login Link: https://socialtrading.mhmarkets.org/portal/login

    • Fill in registration information, then select "Login"
    • Note: The login password must also be the login password of MT4 / MT5

    Subscription Strategy

    Follower must manually activate the subscriptions before activation, including settings:

    • Volume Scaling
    • Compared value:
    • Balance
    • Equity
    • Ratio multiplier

    Volume Scaling

    • Multiply: Adjust the volume of the Follower to be larger or smaller than the original trading volume of the Provider.
    • Auto Scale: The ratio of a copy's volume to the original volume is equal to the ratio of the follower's and provider's Compared values. The result is then multiplied by the Ratio multiplier.
    • Fixed: Set a specific volume regardless of the Provider's volume.

    Example of Volume Scaling

    • Multiply: If Provider opens 1 lot and Follower sets the multiplier to 0.5, Follower's account will open 0.5 lots.
    • Auto Scale: Follower's capital is $1000 and Provider's capital is $10000, ratio 1:10, so Provider's 1 lot will be copied into Follower's 0.1 lot.
    • Fixed: Provider opens 1 lot and Follower sets fixed volume to 2 lots, Follower's account will always open 2 lots.

    Correction

    • Skip:
      - Do not open an order if expected volume exceeds the maximum volume.
    • Scale down:
      - Allows opening an order if expected volume exceeds maximum volume.
    • For example:
      - If the expected volume is 4 lots but the maximum volume is set to 2 lots:
      - Skip: Do not open any volume.
      - Scale down: Open 2 lots.

    Risk Management

    • Stop Loss and Take Profit apply to all copies.
    • Parameters:
      - Total loss
      - Total profit
      - Floating loss

    • Subscription Management:      
      - Suspend
      - Unsubscribe

    • Trade Management:
      - Keep All
      - Close All
      - Close Unprofitable: Close losing orders one by one until account loss is acceptable.
      - Close All Unprofitable: unprofitable transactions

    • Total Loss Level: If PnL reaches the specified loss level, "Close All" or "Suspend" will be triggered.
    • Total Profit Level: If PnL reaches the specified profit level, "Close All" or "Suspend" will be triggered.
    • Floating Loss Level: If PnL is expected to reach the specified floating loss level, "Close All Unprofitable" will be triggered.

    Create Multiple Subscription

    Clients can repeat the registration process to become a Follower (select the required trading account) or operate at “Home” or “My Portfolio”.

    Subscriptions Status

    Displayed on “My Portfolio” page

    • The status of copy subscriptions will be displayed here, making it easy for users to manage and track their subscriptions.

    Unsubscribe / Suspend Manually

    • Click “My Portfolio” > Provider Name

    • At the “Action” button, select “Suspend” or “Unsubscribe”.
    • Confirm the decision to “Suspend” or “Unsubscribe”.
