    Procedures for Chinese Clients – Duplicate Phone Numbers

    Recently, we have encountered several clients registering with the same phone number.

    To ensure smooth onboarding and KYC process, please follow the procedures below based on two possible situations:

    • Situation 1:

    • Situation 2:

    Situation 1:

    • Client-A registered first but has not verified phone number in SumSub (either because they registered before SumSub was implemented or haven't completed verification).

    • Client-B (more recent registrant) is in the process of registration and has already verified their phone number.

    Procedure for Updating Client A's Phone Number

    Step 1: First, click on “Users” in the CRM system.

    Step 2: If Client A is a DC, please select "Trader Users";  if Client A is an IB, please select "Broker User."

    Step 3: After clicking, the following interface will appear. In number 3, choose the method to query the client, such as client's name or email.

    Step 4: After selecting the query method, fill in the client information in number 4. Once completed, click on “Search.”

    Step 5: The corresponding IB information will be displayed. After confirming it’s correct, click the teal “+” icon.

    Step 6: In the “Operation” section, click on “Edit.”

     

    Step 7: You will enter the “Edit” interface, where you can locate the phone number. Before making any changes, please record the original phone number in the  Changing File. After updating the phone number, remember to also update the new number in the  Changing File.

     

    Step 8:  After updating the phone number, click on the “Submit” button.

    Step 9: Next, will let the Client A to proceed with the KYC process. After that, we will recheck the KYC. Please note that the procedures differ for DC and IB clients, as outlined below.

    i) For Direct Client
    ii) For IB

    i) For Direct Client

    • When we click on "sumsub_recheck," the following interface will appear, as shown below.
    • We have to choose the client's level base on requirement (EDD check / China_level_1) and click on "Submit."

     

    ii) For IB

     

    • When we click on "sumsub_recheck," the following interface will appear, as shown below.
    • For IB clients, unlike DC clients, you won’t be able to select the Sumsub applicant level. Instead, the only available options are EDD_Check or MENA_Region_EDD_Check_MU. Therefore, when clicking on "sumsub_recheck," the interface will simply display "Confirm" and "Cancel" buttons.

     

     

     

     

    Situation 2:

    • Client-A has already verified their phone number in SumSub.

    • Client-B is attempting to use the same number during registration.

    The image shows that the same phone number is being used across multiple client accounts during the verification process, indicating that different individuals are attempting to verify their identities using the same number.

    Action:

    • We will reset the phone verification twice for a client. To keep track, we should add tag to client's profile.

     

    • There are two possible outcomes.

    Condition 1 :

    If the client uses different verified phone number, it will be updated in both CRM and Sumsub, and the account opening will proceed as normal.

     

    Condition 2 :

    • If client still use the duplicated number, we will send mail to sale to contact the client by providing client's information. *Please follow the steps in the following link: Side Conversation / Email

    • Add a tag 'Pending AM Contact".

     

    • Once sale successfully contact the client, we can change the client's level to "China Selfie Level".

     

     Phone Verification Failure

    • China_Selfie_Level

    • Clients from China who register through Sales or IB Promotion links

      Situation 1: Sales raises a ticket inquiring about the client's phone verification failure.

      Situation 2: Sales reports in the CS Support Teams group about the client's phone verification failure.

      Situation 3: Client raises a ticket inquiring about the phone verification failure.

       

    China_Selfie_Level

    • If the client under China_Level_1 are unable to receive the SMS verification code (e.g., due to local SMS restrictions), we should change client's level to China_Selfie_Level.
    • Applicable to: China_Level_1 clients who are unable to complete phone verification due to technical or local limitations

      Required verification steps under China_Selfie_Level:
    1. Proof of Identity (POI)
    2. Selfie

     

     

     

    Clients from China who register through Sales or IB Promotion links

    We have updated the Sumsub applicant levels for clients in China who register via Sales or IB promotional links. A new verification level, China_Level_1, has been introduced to enhance compliance procedures.


     

    📌 Required verification steps:

    1. Phone Verification
    2. Proof of Identity (POI)
       

    The client was unable to receive the verification code because of technical issue.

    There are Three possible situations.

    •  Situation 1: Sales raises a ticket inquiring about the client's phone verification failure.
    • Situation 2: Sales reports in the CS Support Teams group about the client's phone verification failure.
    • Situation 3: Client raises a ticket inquiring about the phone verification failure.
       

     Situation 1: Sales raises a ticket inquiring about the client's phone verification failure.

    If client's sale reports that the client cannot receive verification codes, the applicant level can be changed to 'China_Selfie_Level' verification.. By doing this, the client will no longer need to verify their phone number.  

     

     

     

    Situation 2: Sales reports in CS Support Teams group about the client's phone verification failure.

    Solution: If Sales reports in the CS Support Teams group that their client is not receiving the phone verification code, CS will forward the request to us (e.g., by forwarding it to the MHM_Onboarding Microsoft Teams group).

    • We need to ask CS to submit the ticket. We only can proceed the requests come from Zendesk.

     

    Situation 3: Client raises a ticket inquiring about the phone verification failure.

    This situation requires following the procedure as detailed below.

    Step 1 : We'll reset the phone verification so the client can try again.

    Step 2 : If client reports multiple failed phone verification attempts, we will notify the relevant sale from side conservation email, providing the phone number the client submitted for verification in Sumsub. *Please follow the steps in the following link: Side Conversation / Email

    Email template:

    English Version:

    Dear,

    Your client, [Client Name] ([Client Email]), has repeatedly failed phone verification. Please reach out to the client using the following number: [Client's phone number used for verification in Sumsub].

    Once you've successfully contacted the client and confirmed the client available to perform selfie, please let us know, and we'll assist to change his/her applicant verification type to face matching verification (selfie).

    Thank you for your cooperation!

     

    Chinese Version:

    您好，

    您名下客户 [客户姓名] ([客户邮箱]) 手机验证多次失败，劳烦您联系一下。

    请使用该手机号联络客户：[客户在Sumsub用于验证的手机号]

    若您成功联系到客户并确认客户能进行人面识别验证，请告知我们，我们将协助把客户的审核方式调整为人脸识别。

    感谢您的配合！

     

    Step 3: Tag the client's application on SumSub with "Pending AM contact".

     

    Step 4: Once the email is sent, two situations may pop-up:

    a) If sales successfully contact the client, we will change client's applicant level to 'China_Selfie_Level' verification.

    b)  If sales are unable to contact the client, their status will remain as "Phone Verification" pending, and re-attempted verification will be required.

     

    Website Client under SVG

    EDD_check (AMContact)

    1. In some cases, clients under EDD check are unable to receive the SMS verification code (e.g., due to local SMS restrictions) — we will initiate an alternative verification process by adjusting their applicant level to EDD_check (AM Contact).
    2. Before adjusting the client's verification level to EDD_check(AM Contact), it must be confirmed that the Sales has successfully contacted the client.
    3. At this level, the website client will be required to submit the following documents:
    • Proof of Identity (POI)
    • Proof of Address (POA)
    • Selfie

     

    For website clients 

    Currently, all clients registered via the official website under SVG, whose Sumsub Applicant Level is "EDD Check". The clients must complete phone verification during the KYC process.

    📌 Required verification steps:

    1. Phone Verification
    2. Proof of Identity (POI)
    3. Proof of Address (POA)

    4. Selfie

     

    For the Website Client Not Receiving Phone Verification Code during account registration

    Step 1: Obtaining Client Contact Information

    • Confirm the client's country of residence.
    • Include the phone number used for verification in the Sumsub.

    Step 2: Send an email to the assigned account manager based on the client’s country of residency to inform about the phone verification failure: if the client didn't receive the code, OB will inform the sales to contact the client.

    Phone :

    Step 3: Tag the client's application on SumSub with "Pending AM contact".

     

    Step 4: There are two conditions may arise,

    a) Situation 1: The account manager has successfully contacted the client

    b) Situation 2: Failed Client Contact by Account Manager
     

    a) Situation 1: The account manager has successfully contacted the client

    Step 5: Remove the Pending AM contact tag and add a new tag Client successfully contacted.

    Step 6: Update the SumSub applicant level to EDD_check(AMContact).

    📌At this level, the client will be required to submit the following documents:

    • Proof of Identity (POI)
    • Proof of Address (POA)
    • Selfie

     

    b) Situation 2: Failed Client Contact by Account Manager

    Step 5: If sales report their inability to contact the client using Sumsub's registered phone number (indicating potential invalid number), we should maintain the client's status at "Phone Verification" pending and require re-attempted phone verification.

    Step 6: Remove the Pending AM contact tag and add a new tag "Failed Client Contact".
