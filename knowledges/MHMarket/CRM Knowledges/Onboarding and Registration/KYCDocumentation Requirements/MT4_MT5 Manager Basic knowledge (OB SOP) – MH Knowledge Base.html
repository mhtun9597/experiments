    MT4 Manager

    i) Checking the Client's Trading Account Status 

    Step i: First, log in to MT4 Manager.

    Step ii: In "Location 2", enter the client's trading account number and press "Enter."

    Step iii: The system will display the following interface.

    Step iv: The client's details and related trading account information will be shown.

    Step v: "Balance" is used to check the trading account's balance. If "Balance" does not match "Equity," it indicates that there are currently open positions in the trading account.

     

    ii) Check if there are any pending orders in the trading account.

    The photo below shows the pending orders. Transactions listed above the trading account status are Opened Orders, while those below are Pending Orders.

     

    iii) Checking the Client's Trading History

    Step 1: Click on "History."

    Step 2: Then, the interface below will pop up.

    Step 3: The period can be selected according to your preference.

    Step 4: Then, click "ok"

    Step 5: Detailed information will be displayed, including the products the client traded, the lot sizes, and other relevant details.

     

    MT5 Manager

    i) Checking the Client's Trading Account Status
    Step i: First, log in to MT5 Manager.

    Step ii & iii: In the "Location 2", enter the client's trading account number and click the "Search" icon.

    Step iv: The system will display the following interface.

    Step v: The client's details and related trading account information will be shown.

    Step vi: "Balance" is used to check the trading account's balance. If "Balance" does not match "Equity," it indicates that there are currently open positions in the trading account.

     

    ii) Checking the Client's Trading History

    Step 1: Click on "History"

    Step 2: Click on position 2, and the interface below will pop up. From there, you can select the period based on your preference.

    Step 3: Then, click on "Request."

    Step 4: Detailed information will be displayed, including the products the client traded, the lot sizes, and other relevant details.
