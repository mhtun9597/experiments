    CRM Step by Step Guide: User Tab and What it is useful for

    Arion
    January 20, 2026 06:48 Updated

    In this article we will review Users tab in the CRM system and what it can be used for:

    •  Users tab
      • Trade User
      • Broker User
      • MT Accounts
      • Competitions Accounts
      • Team Members
      • Wallet Address
      • Relationship Change Record
      • Promotion Links
      • Sales Commissions
      • Account Openings Inquiry
    Trade User – Used to view and monitor trader information, including MT Server, Username, Superior Agent, Account Manager (AM), Email, and Mobile Number.
    • Reset CRM/Profile password for a client
    • Unlock CRM/Profile access for a client
    Broker User - To monitor and check client (IB) information and details such as MT Server,
    Real name, Parent, AM, Role, Tier, Email and Mobile number.
    • Reset CRM/Profile password for IB
    • Check client’s IB under with sales team or RGM.
      1. Click – Users > Broker User
      2. Type in client’s (IB) Email /Name
      3. It will show client’s RGM/ Team
    MT Accounts - To Monitor and check client/user MT account information and details such as
    MT Server, Superior agent, Real name, Account name, Trading account ID, Spread Rebate
    group, Account Group, Deposit group, Account type, Leverage, account opening time, AM,
    email, mobile number
    • Check client’s account balance information and account trading function
      Enable or Disable in this tab.
    • Reset ‘’Trading Account Password’’ for CLIENTS
    Competitions Accounts - (2023 END) To monitor and check trading contest users account information
    and details such as MT Server, account name, real name, trading account id, spread rebate
    group Deposit Group, Account Type and leverage.

    Team Members - To check internal team members information and details.

    Wallet Address - To monitor and check client’s wallet address details and status.

    Relationship Change Record - No access
    Demo Accounts - To monitor and check client/user demo accounts details

    Promotion Links - To check promotion links information and details.

    Sales Commissions - To check internal sales team information and details

    Account Openings Inquiry - To monitor and check IB Registration and Account Opening
    Inquiry & TA Registration and Account Opening Inquiry. 

    If client mentioned done register, but we didn't find any record on Account Opening Audits, we can try to find it here. If it shows in Account Opening Inquiry, can refer the status and let the relevant department or sales to know the register's status.
