    KYC Guidelines: Accepted Proof of Identity Documents by Country

    KSLIM (OB)
    January 12, 2026 08:49 Updated

    As part of our due diligence procedure, we must verify the authenticity and validity of identity documents. The common standard for KYC requires to clearly display all of the following details below on the identity documents and must be within the validity period. Please process the KYC approval as usual when the identity documents submitted by the client adhere to the following common standards:

    • Client's full name
    • Date of birth
    • ID Document number
    • Issuing governmental authority and department
    • ID Document issuance date
    • ID Document expiry date or validity date

    General Rules for Accepted Documents:

    • The font and the individual’s photo must be clear and legible.
    • The residence address and the issuing governmental authority must match.
    • The ID document must be current and within its validity period.
    • For Chinese IDs, ensure the wording indicates a real “Resident” status (refer to the screenshots above for examples).
