    CRM Step by Step Guide: Audit Management and What it is useful for

    Arion
    January 20, 2026 06:06 Updated

    In this article we will review an Audit tab in the CRM system and what it can be used for:

    • Checking client's PROFILE status
      • Account opening statuses explained
    • Checking client's INTERNAL TRANSFER status
      • Transaction Application Status:
    • Checking client's WITHDRAW status
      • Transaction Review progress statuses explained
    • Checking client's LEVERAGE CHANGING APPLICATION
    • Checking SALES PROMOTION LNK SUBMISSION
    • Checking SALES COMMISSION

    Checking client's PROFILE status

    When a client mentions that they have completed the account registration but haven't received a confirmation email

    Check on CRM whether client have successful to register:

    1. Click - Audit Management 
    2. Click - Account Opening Audit
    3. Type in Client’s Name or email address
    4. Click ‘’Search’’
    5. Check on the client’s application status
    6. If show ‘’Finish’’, let client know the status. If ‘’Awaiting Documents’’, escalation to Onboarding team to assist.

    If the client details are not visible in the Account Opening Audit:

    • Click - Users
    • Click - Account Opening Inquiry
    • Click - TA Registration and Account Opening Inquiry
    • Change the type you want to search and type Email/Name
    • Click ‘’Search’’
    • Refer to the Account Opening status for the reason. If the client is on the list, it means the registration was not complete yet.

    Account opening statuses explained

    There are four status options displayed in the Account Opening Status:

    Checking client's INTERNAL TRANSFER status

    Reminder
    Before providing client sensetive information such as transacytion status and details, make sure the client request is coming from Authorised experience: it will have GREEN tick.
    1. Click - Audit Management 
    2. Click - Transfer Audit 
    3. Type in Client’s MT Account Number/Name or Email
    4. Click ‘’Search’’
    5. Check on the client’s application status
    6. If show ‘’Finish’’, let client know the status. If ‘’Pending’’, escalation to Funding team request for checking.

    Transaction Application Status:

    There are 5 possible statuses displayed:

    1. Pending: The client application has been received.
    2. Processing: The client application is currently being reviewed by the Funding team.
    3. Reject: The client application has been declined.
    4. Finish: The client application has been successfully finalized.
    5. Client Termination: The client application has been canceled by the client.

    Checking client's DEPOSIT status.

    Reminder
    Before providing client sensetive information such as transacytion status and details, make sure the client request is coming from Authorised experience: it will have GREEN tick.

    Check client whether have successful submit deposit request.

    1. Click - Audit Management 
    2. Click - Deposit Audit 
    3. Type in client’s MT Account Number /Name
    4. Click ‘’Search’’
    5. Check on the client’s application status
    6. If show ‘’Finish’’, let client know the status. If ‘’Pending’’, escalation to Funding team request for checking.

    Transaction Application Status:

    There are 5 possible statuses displayed:

    1. Pending: The client application has been received.
    2. Processing: The client application is currently being reviewed by the Funding team.
    3. Reject: The client application has been declined.
    4. Finish: The client application has been successfully finalized.
    5. Client Termination: The client application has been canceled by the client.

    Checking client's WITHDRAW status

    Reminder
    Before providing client sensetive information such as transacytion status and details, make sure the client request is coming from Authorised experience: it will have GREEN tick.

    Check client whether have successful submit withdrawal request.

    1. Click - Audit Management 
    2. Click - Withdrawal Fund Audit 
    3. Type in client’s MT Account Number /Name or Email
    4. Click ‘’Search’’
    5. Check on the client’s application status
    6. If show ‘’Finish’’, let client know the status. If ‘’Pending’’, escalation to Funding team request for checking.

    Transaction Review progress statuses explained

    • Fund Pending - Initial step
    • Fund Pass - Processing - The withdrawal has been processed by the Funding department
    • Fund Reject - Reject - The application have been rejected by the Funding department
    • Risk Pass - Processing - The risk control department has completed the review
    • Risk Hold - Processing - Risk control is on hold
    • Risk Reject - Reject - The application have been rejected by the Risk Control department
    • Deduction Hold - Processing - Deduction is on hold (include reason)
    • Deduction Pass - Remittance in Progress - Deduction has been made
    • Deduction Pass - Remitted - Funds have been remitted
    • Deduction Reject - Reject - The application has been rejected by the Funding department after successfully passed risk control.

    Note: Please refer to the Handle feedback for the specific reason.

    Checking client's LEVERAGE CHANGING APPLICATION

    1. Click - Audit Management
    2. Click -Leverage Audit
    3. Search client’s detail – Name/Email/MT Account Number

    Checking SALES PROMOTION LNK SUBMISSION

    • Click - Audit Management
    • Click - Open Links
    • Type in Client’s Email Address and Search
    • Refer to the Approval status.

    Checking SALES COMMISSION

    • Click - Audit Management
    • Click - Sales Commission
    • Type in email and Search
    • Refer to the review progress and application status.
    • Review Progress status are the same as withdraw progress.
