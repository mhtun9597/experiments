    Guide on Changing Client's Registered Email and Phone Number in Sumsub

    CS Assistant
    February 04, 2026 07:49 Updated

    The below information provides step by step guidance on how to change email and phone for clients' information in SumSub. 

    Important note:
    Always update the details in CRM first. Once the CRM reflects the correct information, you can proceed to make changes in Sumsub. If you update Sumsub before CRM, the data will not sync back to CRM, causing inconsistencies.
    1. Login to SumSub Cockpit using your email and password (no SSO)
    2.  Find the IB/Client's registered email address in the "Applicant Information" section.
    3.  Next, change the email address.
    4.  After that, find the IB/Client's registered phone number in the "Personal info" section.
    5. Next, we change the phone number.
    6. Then Click "Save"
      • Add "change mail" tag.
    7. Phone number should be changed both in the "Profile information" and "Personal info", 
      • go to Personal information Application Information > click Edit
      • Enter new phone and click "save changes" 
      • go to Personal information section and click "Edit"
      • Enter new phone and click "save changes" 
    When entering phone number make sure to enter in the following format: +(countru code)followed by the phone number.

    Ater changing client's registered email and phone number, the client may resubmit the documents.
