    Corporate Account Application (OB SOP)

    KSLIM (OB)
    January 08, 2026 09:31 Updated

    We can proceed with a corporate account opening request regardless of whether it is submitted by the client directly or by a sales representative.

    1. Verify Previous Applications:
      • If the client has previously registered a corporate account, they can still apply for another corporate account under a different company name.
        Example: A client with an account under Company A can apply for another account under Company B.
    2. Request Required Documents:
      • Basic Required Documents:
        • Valid ID (Passport/Driver’s License/National ID) for all beneficiaries and directors.
        • Certificate of Directors.
        • Certificate of Shareholders/Members.
        • Certificate of Incorporation.
      • Advanced Optional Documents:
        • Certificate of Good Standing.
        • Proof of Residence (Utility Bill/Bank Statement issued within 3 months).
        • Registered Office proof (e.g., bank statement in company name showing registered address).
    3. Verify Company Information Online:
      • Use trusted sources (company databases, news articles, official registries) via Google to cross-check legitimacy.
    4. Review Submitted Documents:
      • Situation 1: All documents submitted → Proceed.
      • Situation 2: Missing documents → Request missing items.
      • Situation 3: Unclear or questionable documents → Forward to Compliance for review.
    5. Check CRM for Existing Account:
      • Situation A: Client wants to convert an existing account to corporate → Update name and upload documents.
      • Situation B: Client previously registered as IB and wants another corporate account →
        • Submit KYC via Sumsub (UBO’s personal ID only).
        • Use a different email from the existing account.
        • After Sumsub approval, create account in CRM and update corporate details.
      • Situation C: Client has no prior IB registration →
        • Submit KYC via Sumsub (UBO’s personal ID only).
        • After approval, create account in CRM and update corporate details.
    6. Update CRM Account Name:
      • Change account name to reflect company name.
    7. Upload Required Documents to CRM:
      • Ensure all documents are uploaded and verified.

    Step-by-Step Guide in CRM

    Update Client Name in CRM

    1. Click Users → Broker User.
    2. Search for client by name or email.
    3. Click the green "+" symbol.
    4. Click Edit.
    5. Update Real Name and English Name fields.

    6. Click Submit.

    Upload Documents to CRM

    1. Click Users → Broker User.
    2. Search for client by name or email.
    3. Click the green "+" symbol.
    4. Click File Management → Upload.
    5. Verify all required documents are uploaded correctly.
