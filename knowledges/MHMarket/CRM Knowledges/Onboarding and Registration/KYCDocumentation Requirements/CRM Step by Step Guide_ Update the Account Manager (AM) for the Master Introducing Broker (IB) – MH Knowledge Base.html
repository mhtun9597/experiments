    CRM Step by Step Guide: Update the Account Manager (AM) for the Master Introducing Broker (IB)

    CS Assistant
    March 03, 2026 07:46 Updated

    This article is related to the Change Account Manager (OB SOP). Below is a detailed, step-by-step guide on how to update the Account Manager (AM) for the Master Introducing Broker (IB), in cases where the change has been requested and approved for the client. 

    There could be two scenarios: 

    1. There are clients under the transferring IB 
    2. There are NO clients under the transferring IB
    Make sure client sends email from his registered email address.
    1. First, click on “Users” in the CRM system.
    2.  Under the “Users” option, click on “Broker User".
    3. After clicking, the following interface will appear. In position 3, select the method to search for the client, such as by client name or identification code. Fill in the client information.
    4. Once completed, click on "Search."
    5. The corresponding client information will be displayed; then click on the green "+" symbol.
    6. Next, click on the "Rebate Settings" symbol.
    7. Go to the "Rebate Settings" interface and remove the original sales.
    8. Then, enter the name of the new sales member in the "Parent" field.
    9. The corresponding sales information will be displayed.
    10. Click on the new sales you want to select, and then the selected sales will be display.
    11. After confirming everything is correct, click "Submit."
    Important If a new sub-IB got transferred under sale, IB's role will be changed to MIB. Additionally, all Sub IBs under the transferred IB must also be adjusted accordingly

    Scenario 2.1: There are clients under the transferring

    We need to confirm the rebate structure for the entire tree line due to the level changes.

    1. Once the sales replies to the ticket and provides the rebate template, we can set up the rebate for the new IB.
      Please follow the steps in the following link: How to set rebate for an IB in "Broker Users" (OB SOP)
    2. After setting up the rebate for the new IB, we can set the IB as MIB under the new sales member's name by recording the changes in the Changing File.
    3. Once the setup is complete, we can respond to the ticket.
    Please follow the steps in the following link to respond to the ticket: Macro Reply

    Scenario 2.2: There are no clients under the transferring IB

    We can move the IB under Sales by setting the rebate to full and recording the change in the Changing File.

    1. When replying to Sales, please mention that the IB was transferred with full rebate for all account types. (How to set rebate for an IB in "Broker Users" (OB SOP)
    2. Once the setup is complete, we can respond to the ticket.
    Please follow the steps in the following link to respond to the ticket: Macro Reply
