    Create Ticket

    How to create a ticket and request confirmation from the sales :

    Step 1: Go to "Zendesk" and click "Add."

    Step 2: Then select "Ticket."

    Step 3: Fill in your email address in the "Requester" section.

    Step 4: In the "Assignee" field, select a CS team member who is currently online (indicated by a green mark).

    Step i) : Click on the "Assignee" field, and the interface below will appear. From there, select "Customer Support Group."

    Step ii: Next, the interface below will appear, displaying a list of CS members. Choose only "Customer Assistant".

     

    Step 5: In the "Tag" field, enter "onboarding." The interface below will appear. Select "tagging onboarding" from the options.

     

    Step 6: In the "Theme" field, Select "Account Management" from the options.

     

    Step 7: After selecting the "Account Management" as theme, the interface below will appear. Select "Rebate Setting" or choose relevant one from the options.

     

    Step 8: In the "Internal note," write the reason of ticket creation and client [Name] (Email)."

    Step 9: Then click "Submit as New."

     

     

    Side Conversation / Email

    How to send email using side conversation:

    After creating the ticket, proceed to send an email within the Ticket to the client's sales to inquire the rebate template for the client's IB.

     

    Step i: Click the "+" next to "Side conversations" in the upper right corner of the ticket (as shown in the image above).

     

     

    Step ii: Enter the original sales email address (do not enter the sales account name, as the system will not recognize it, and the original sales will not receive the email).

    Step iii: Set the email subject to "Rebate Confirmation."

    Step iv: Kindly edit the email content using the template provided in the following link : Email Template

    Step v: Once everything is filled out, click "Send."

    Step vi: After the email is sent, it will appear as shown in the image below.

     

     

    Macro Reply

    How to reply ticket using Macro:

    Step i: Click on "Apply macro."

     

    Step ii: In "All," click on "Onboarding Team."

     

    Step iii: Then, under the "Onboarding Team" options, select "Ticket is Solved, Send back to Agent to notify client."

     

    Step iv: Next, the following content will appear in the "Internal Note."

    Step v: Click "Submit as Processed. To notify client" to reply to the CS.

     

     

    Revert back to CS

    How to revert back to CS Team

    Step i: Click "Apply macro."

     

    Step ii：In "All," click on "Onboarding Team."

    Step iii: Then, under the "Onboarding Team" option, select "More Inquiries from CS."

    Step iv: Next, the following content will appear in the "Internal Note." Here, you can enter the matters we need to confirm with sales.

    Step v: Click "Submit as Need Feedback from CS" to reply to customer service. Once received, customer service will confirm with sales.

     

     

    Escalate to relevant team

    How to escalate to relevant team using Macro :

    Step i: Click "Apply micro" on the ticket sent by the client.

     

    Step ii: Click "Onboarding Team"

     

    Step iii: Then, select "Onboarding to Reporting Team" or relevant team we want to escalate for under the "Onboarding Team" option.

     

    Step iv: Next, the following content will appear in the "Internal Note."

    Step v: Click "Submit."

     

    Step vi: The Reporting Team will respond to the ticket after processing.
