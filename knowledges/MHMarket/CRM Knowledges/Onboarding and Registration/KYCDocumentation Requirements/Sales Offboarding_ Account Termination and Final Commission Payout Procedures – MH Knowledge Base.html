    Sales Offboarding: Account Termination and Final Commission Payout Procedures

    CS Assistant
    January 13, 2026 13:28 Updated

    The purpose of this SOP is to define the standardized process for offboarding resigned sales employees and ensuring accurate and timely final commission payouts. This procedure aims to maintain operational integrity, safeguard CRM data, and provide resigned employees with a clear and secure method to withdraw their final commissions within the stipulated timeframe.

    Scope

    This SOP applies to HR, Customer Service (CS), Onboarding, Data Analysis (DA), Risk Control (RC), Operations (OP), Funding, and Finance teams involved in the offboarding and commission payout process.

    Onboarding, Customer Service and Funding Team Please ensure that we are ONLY following the new procedures when comes to processing sales final commission payout request.

    Sales Offboarding Process

    1. When HR receives a Sales resignation, HR will send an email to the Customer Service team with the list of clients under the resigned Sales representative, to be transferred to an assigned AM, including a request to set up 7-day CRM access.
    2. CS Team escalates internally:
      • Escalate To Onboarding Team (Internal Note): For CRM offboarding and client reassignment.
      • Escalate To Data Analysis Team: For commission calculation.
    3. Onboarding Team Restricts access to only wallet and withdrawal feature. 
      • Reassigns clients according to internal SOP.
    4. Onboarding Team Confirms completion in the ticket.
    5. Data Analysis Team Actions Calculates final commissions.
    6. Data Analysis Team send email with results with Risk Control (RC) 
    7. Once RC approves, DA updates the ticket and notifies CS
    8. CS escalates the ticket to Operations Team for Crediting the approved commission amount to the employee’s sales wallet.
    9.  Operations Team Credits commission to the wallet.
    10. CS Updates HR 
      • CS informs HR that commission crediting is complete.
      • Marks ticket as Solved.
      • HR will inform the resigned Sales representative that they are eligible to withdraw their commission.
    11. Employee logs into CRM portal within the 7-day access window. During this period, access to all other CRM information will remain restricted. Users will have access to their own sales wallet and withdrawal request submission feature only 
      • Submits withdrawal application via standard process.
    12. Funding Team processes withdrawal request according to usual withdrawal procedures.
    13. Performs fund remittance following standard remittance procedures.
    14. Once the HR resignation request is submitted, the account will be automatically closed on the 8th day from that date.
