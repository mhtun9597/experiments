    MH Markets KYC Recheck Policy

    Willow (OB)
    February 04, 2026 06:32 Updated

    Upon specific criteria, we can initiate an EDD KYC Recheck policy, in which case a client will be required to provide POI, Selfie and in some cases undergo Phone verification. We can monitor the client's progress in SumSub. If the client submits all required documents, our system will automatically approve the application. Before the KYC Submission is successful, the client cannot operate the following actions:

    • Internal transfer requests
    • Same-name account applications
    • Withdrawal requests

    There is a number of reasons why company can initiate a KYC recheck process:

    • RC & Compliance required
    • Entity Changing (MAU and SVG)
    • Expired documents (ID)
    • Suspicious Financial activity
    • USDT Withdrawal Selfie Step (Email Verification – Manual Trigger)
    • USDT Withdrawal Selfie Step (System Auto-Detection – Already Live in CRM)
    • Suspicious Found on Individual Clients
    • Suspicious IB and Sales Clients KYC Recheck Policy
    • Suspicious IBs' and Sales' Client
    • How to setup KYC Recheck to specific sales?
    • How to setup KYC Recheck to specific IBs?

    Expired ID

    • Since our Sumsub system has ongoing monitoring feature, once the client's submitted ID is expired, it will be changed to resubmission requested on Sumsub and client will be required to provide an update one.

    Financial Activity Triggers:

    Suspicious Financial activity

    If a client has previously submitted a withdrawal request which was later canceled by the client (Client Termination), and subsequently changed their USDT address, or changed their registered email followed by a recent change of USDT address, this will trigger an enhanced KYC level.

    • Once triggered, the client must complete the KYC process via the Client Portal, which includes the following requirements: 
      1. Proof of Identity (POI)
      2. Liveness Selfie
      3. Phone Verification

    USDT Withdrawal Selfie Step (Email Verification – Manual Trigger)

    • If the system is unable to automatically detect the necessary verification elements, an email will be sent by Funding team requesting the client to manually complete the USDT withdrawal selfie step.

    USDT Withdrawal Selfie Step (System Auto-Detection – Already Live in CRM)

    • When a client links a USDT address via the Client Portal, and that USDT address is detected in CRM as being shared with other client, this KYC condition will be triggered.
      In this case, the client must complete KYC in the Client Portal by submitting: 
      1. Proof of Identity (POI)
      2. Liveness Selfie

    Currently, clients who deposit in local currencies and withdraw via USDT undergo manual email-based KYC verification by FD team member during withdrawal review.

    Suspicious Found on Individual Clients

    If we are suspicious that the client has a risk by making a large amount of deposit, we can enhance the KYC requirements to the specific client.

    Suspicious IB and Sales Clients KYC Recheck Policy

    1. EDD KYC (New Registration)
      – This option applies to "all new clients" register under the selected "Sales" or "IBs".
      – All upcoming clients registered under that specific person will need to make EDD Verification. (EDD_Check)
    2. EDD KYC (Existing Clients)
      – This option applies to "all existing clients" register under the selected "Sales" or "IBs".
      – All current clients will be requested to do EDD Verification when they login to their dashboard. (EDD_Check)
