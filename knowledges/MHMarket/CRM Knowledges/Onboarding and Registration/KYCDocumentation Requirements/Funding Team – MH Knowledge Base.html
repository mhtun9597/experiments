    Responsibilities:

    Ensure the seamless management of customer funds while delivering an excellent service experience. Additionally, adhere to company regulations to identify, assess, and mitigate risks associated with customer funds, thereby eliminate/minimizing potential risks.

    ​
    ​Deposit

    • Deposit Time： 24/7 Automatically Credited
    • Payment Channels - The service hours of the third-party deposit channels are different, and the deposit will be adjusted according to the situation of each channel in the early morning. ​

    Deposit Group​

    • ​Clients are assigned in their respective deposit group​
    • Website Client – No payment Channels​

    Deposit Limit​

    • ​First Deposit limit  - ​ STD- 50 /Cent- 10 USD​ / ECN-1000​ /Prime-100​
    • Payment Channel Deposit Limit - ​ ​For details, please refer to the channel deposit instructions.  ​
    • USDT - ​ No limit (removed in CRM 2/5/2025)​  (depends on your wallet limit)​
    • Wire Transfer【Equal Money】- ​ Minimum 1000 USD ; Max -100,000 (CRM Limit)​
    • Mashreq Bank Wire AED​ - ​ Minimum  500 USD as of 2/18/2025 ; Max- 100,000 (CRM Limit)
    • Visa/Mastercard【MyFatoorah】AED​ - ​ Minimum 182 AED (50 USD CRM Limit)​ ; Max- 1,000,000 AED  (Cardholders Limit)​

    Deposits more than the limit :​   Client need to contact us in order to check which payment channels can accommodate such request.​

    ​

    Deposit Fee​

    • No Deposit Fee ​The company cover all handling fee
    • Visa/Mastercard ​(Deposited and Withdrawal Fee waived 2/3/2025)​
    • Exception fee’s deducted ：​

    - Bank processing fee – Wire Transfer​

    - Network Fee - USDT (Platform Fee like Binance)​

    • ​Visa/Master Invoice Deposit Procedure Update​

    To process invoice deposits, kindly follow these updated requirements:​

    ​Required Documents​ Clients must provide a color copy of both the front and back sides of their card, ensuring the following details are visible:​

    •  
      • Full name of the cardholder​
      • Last 4 digits of your card number​
      • Card expiry date​

    Security Recommendations​

    For security purposes, all digits except the last 4 on the front side of the card should be covered.​
    The 3-digit CVV code on the back of the card must be covered (we do not require the CVV code).​
    All other required details must be clear and visible.​

    *Note: Encouragement for CRM Deposits​
    To streamline the process and enhance security, we recommend encouraging clients to submit deposit orders directly through the CRM whenever possible and avoid using invoice deposits unless absolutely necessary.​

    ​Deposit Issue​

    Manual Process:​

    • ​System Failures: ​
    • Missing Reference Number​
    • Client Confirmation: Clients forget to click the ‘I have paid’ button​
    • Transaction Hash (USDT Deposits)​
    • System Delays – High Volume​
    • Third party deposits-We do not accept deposits from third party. When you deposit funds, the bank account name must be the same as the registration name.​
    • Client deposit the funds to the Wrong account. ​
    • Client deposit the funds to the Wrong Amount. ​

     

    ​What to do?​

    • ​Clients should not re-register again​
    • Client should contact CS/Sales/IB​

    The process usually works like this:​

    1. ​Ask for information – Account number/Email and proof of deposit ​
    2. Payment Channel Verification​
    3. Approval and Crediting:​

    ​Client Transfer Incorrect Amount​

    • Online Payment- ​

    Fund transfer more than required amount ​

    Client need to provide bank information for refund​ or Client register new deposit request with the equivalent amount​

    Fund transfer less than required amount

    ​Client can top up the remaining/missing amount​ or Register new deposit request with the equivalent amount based on the current deposit exchange rate)​

    • ​USDT​

    Fund transfer more than required amount ​Check with Technical team, once confirm create supplementary deposit request​

    Fund transfer less than required amount ​Check with Technical team, once confirm it will treated as deposit fee and the actual amount will be credited )​

    • ​Wire Transfer​

    Actual amount receive will be credited ​

    Withdrawal Limit:​

    Minimum Amount​
