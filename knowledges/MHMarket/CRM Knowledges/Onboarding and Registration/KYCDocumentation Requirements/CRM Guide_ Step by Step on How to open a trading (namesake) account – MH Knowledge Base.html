    CRM Guide: Step by Step on How to open a trading (namesake) account

    Customer Service
    January 19, 2026 10:08 Updated

    When clients use a Direct Client Promotion Link or an IB Promotion Link, it is typically necessary to open an account after the KYC process (What is KYC?) is completed. This account is a trading account for Direct Clients (DCs) and a commission account for Introducing Brokers (IBs). Additionally, for IBs, an extra trade account may be required depending on the type of promotion link they use.

    In certain instances, sales representatives or agents may request the creation of a namesake account via email or ticket. When such a request is made, the account must be manually opened from the backend Customer Relationship Management (CRM) system.

    Step by Step CRM Guide: Opening Trading Additional Trading Account

    Step 1 : We need to go "MT Accounts" of "Users".

    Step 2 : After clicking, the following interface will appear. In position 2, select a method to query the customer, such as by customer name or email. After selecting the query method, fill in the client's details.

    Step 3: Once completed, click on "Search." The corresponding client information will be displayed. In the "Real Name" section. 

    Step 4 : We need to click on "Switch View".

    Step 5: After that, you will return to the following interface, where we need to add a trading account for the IB. Click on "Add."

    Below box will appear.

    Step 6: If the MT Account is for MT4, you can only select MT4 in the "MT Server"; if it's for MT5, you can only select MT5.

    Here are the trading groups for MT4 to choose "MT Platform".

    Step 7: In the "Account Group," if you are opening an STD, Prime, or ECN account, select "Trade Account"; if you are opening Cent account, select "Cent". If the IB rebate template displayed earlier indicates "STD," it means the IB needs to open an STD trading account. Therefore, select "Trade Account" in the "Account Group."

    Step 8: Then, select "STD" in the "Account Type."

    Step 9: In the "Spread Rebate Method," multiple rebate groups will be displayed. Please refer to the details below. (i, ii & iii)

    i)

    ii) Here, the trading account groups are displayed (STD, ECN, Prime, and Cent).

    iii) Different trading accounts have varying spreads, requiring different trading groups. For more details, please refer to the trading account spread table.

    Step 10: Select 1:2000 in the "Leverage" section.

    Step 11: In the "Rebate Settlement Method," select "$."

    Step 12: After completing the setup, please confirm that the information are correct. Once verified, click “Submit.”
