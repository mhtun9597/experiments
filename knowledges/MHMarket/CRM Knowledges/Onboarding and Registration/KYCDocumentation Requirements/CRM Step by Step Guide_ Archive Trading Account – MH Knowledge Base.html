    CRM Step by Step Guide: Archive Trading Account

    CS Assistant
    March 03, 2026 08:41 Updated

     Step 1 : We need to go "MT Accounts" of "Users".

    Step 2 : After clicking, the following interface will appear. In position 2, select a method to query the customer, such as by customer name or email. After selecting the query method, fill in the client's details.

    Step 3: Once completed, click "Search." The corresponding client information will be displayed.

    Step 4: Select the "Archived" option to view archived accounts.

    Step 5 : The archived account will appear. Click on the "backUpAcc" icon to begin the restoration process.

    Step 6: After that, the interface shown above will appear. Click "Confirm" to proceed with restoring the archived account.
