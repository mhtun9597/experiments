    Account Opening Review(OB SOP)

    KSLIM (OB)
    January 28, 2026 07:44 Updated
    Once a client’s KYC documents have been approved by Sumsub, the next stage is the Account Opening Review. Our system can now perform this review automatically. If the system cannot complete the review, a manual review will be required.
    Note Website clients always require a manual review by our team (Level 3 EDD Check).
    1. Log in to the CRM system.
    2. Click on Client Management > KYC Audit
    3. In Audit Management, choose Account Opening Audit.
    4. Search for Client Information
    5. If it shows Sumsub Approved, you can proceed with the application.
    6. For additional details, refer to Sumsub.

    Application Status: For further information, please refer to Sumsub

    SumSub Application Status and Definition

    At the same time, we need to confirm the type of application. The application types are as the following:

    i. TA Account Opening - Client applying for a trading account

    ii. IB Account Opening - Client applying to become an IB through an agent link

    iii. Namesake Account Opening - Clients/IBs who have already opened accounts

    Please confirm the above information on the client's application type and proceed with the account opening according to the following steps.

    *Please refer to the processes for A, B, and C
