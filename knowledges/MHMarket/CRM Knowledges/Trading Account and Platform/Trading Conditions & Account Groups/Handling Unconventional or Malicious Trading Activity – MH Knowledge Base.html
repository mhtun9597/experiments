    Handling Unconventional or Malicious Trading Activity

    Kuek
    January 13, 2026 09:24 Updated

    This article outlines the steps and procedures to follow when a client is suspected of engaging in malicious or unconventional trading activities, such as scalping or exploiting abnormal trading profits.

    Applicable to all departments involved in client communication regarding unconventional trading alerts, including Risk (RC), Customer Service (CS), Sales, and Regional General Managers (RGM).

    • Internal Handling Instructions
    • Account Termination
      • Important Notes
    • Email Templates

    Internal Handling Instructions

    1. RC provides an email template to CS indicating unconventional trading activity in a specific client account.
    2. CS to send the following emails to the clients listed, and blind copy (BCC) their direct agent, sales representative, RGM, and BD. (Important: Please make sure to use BCC.)
      • Attach the Client Acknowledge Form in the email and include KYC requirements. (Profits can be deducted immediately upon signing the agreement / deductions will be made after 7 days if no agreement is signed.)
      • Require the client to sign the Violation Agreement or wait 7 days for the illegal profits to be deducted.
      • If the agent and the client are the same person, you may combine both notifications into one email.
    It is very important to use BCC (Blind Carbon Copy). this email feature allows the sender to include recipients without revealing their email addresses to each other. This is different from CC (Carbon Copy), where all recipients can see who else received the email.
    1. Disable the client’s withdrawal and trading permissions for all accounts under their name.
    Note:
    Even if there are open positions, trading permissions must remain disabled.
    1.  Deduct the illegal rebate amount (USD ***) from the agent’s account immediately.
      • If the agent’s wallet balance is insufficient, temporarily disable withdrawal permissions until the deduction is complete.
    2. Mark the client as a violating user in the CRM system (handled by Risk Control).
    3. Open Positions
      • If the client has open positions:
        • Temporary trading access may be granted for position closing upon request.
        • If positions are not closed within 7 days, the system will forcibly liquidate them and deduct illegal profits.

    ***NOTE: The flow chart is for those clients who will be fully terminated. If RC instructs that we would only need to terminate specific trade account ONLY, then we don't need to send to OB for user termination. Instead, please check with OP to close all the access of that specific trading account (deposit/withdrawal/trade/login).

    Account Termination

    • After the client withdraws their principal, permanently disable CRM access.
    • Accounts with illegal profit deductions cannot have trading permissions reactivated.
    • If the client wishes to continue cooperation, a new account may only be opened with BD or RGM approval.

    Important Notes

    • Always BCC the agent, sales representative, RGM, and BD when sending client emails.
    • If the agent and client are the same person, combine notifications into one email.
    • If RC instructs termination of a specific trade account only: Do not send to OB for full user termination. Instead, coordinate with OP to close all access for that specific account (deposit/withdraw/trade/login).
    • KYC verification is mandatory before re-enabling withdrawal permissions.
    • Accounts marked as terminated cannot regain trading access.
    • New accounts for terminated clients require BD/RGM approval.

    Email Templates

    Client Email

    Subject: Notice on Handling of Abnormal Trading Activity in Account {***}

    Body: Dear Client,

    Following an investigation by our upstream service provider, your account {***} has been found to engage in scalping and obtain abnormal trading profits through non-compliant activity. This violates our trading regulations and fair trading principles.

    To maintain market integrity, the following measures apply:

    • Illegal profit of USD *** will be deducted from your account.
    • Trading and withdrawal permissions have been disabled until processing is complete.
    • If you have open positions, contact Customer Service within 1 hour for temporary access to close them. Otherwise, positions will be forcibly liquidated after 7 days.

    Please sign and return the Violation Agreement within 7 days.

    • If signed: Withdrawal permissions will be restored after deduction.
    • If not signed: Profits will be deducted, but withdrawal permissions remain disabled until agreement is signed.

    Sincerely,
    MH Markets
    (Attach Decay Analysis Report and Client Acknowledge Form)

    Agent Email

    Subject: Notice of Rebate Commission Deduction Due to Client’s Abnormal Trading Activity

    Body: Dear Partner,

    The client {***} under your IB tree-line has been identified as engaging in scalping and abnormal trading behavior. A deduction of USD *** will be made from your wallet {IB’s name (code)}.

    If your wallet balance is insufficient, withdrawal and internal transfer permissions will be temporarily disabled until the full amount is deducted.

    Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
