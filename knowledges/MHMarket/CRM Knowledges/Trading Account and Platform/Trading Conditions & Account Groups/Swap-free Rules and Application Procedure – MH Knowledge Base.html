    Swap-free Rules and Application Procedure

    Yuco
    April 13, 2026 05:55 Updated

    This SOP outlines the swap-free account policies for clients based on their region, eligibility verification, application procedure, and compliance monitoring. Swap-free status is granted mainly to Muslim clients, with exceptions for Indonesian clients. The process involves user verification, eligibility confirmation, escalation to Reporting, enabling swap-free status, and client notification. Misuse or abusive trading can lead to permanent revocation without notice. Thailand has specific exception handling and approval requirements.

    Scope

    This SOP applies to the Customer Support Team, Operations Team, Risk & Compliance Team, and Trading Operations. It covers client eligibility, approval criteria, account activation and deactivation, swap charging logic, and monitoring and misuse handling.

    Swap Free by Region

    Based on the client's region, the following rules will apply:

    Note: Cent account is not applicable.
    • Each trade position will be swap-free for 7 days, with swap fees applicable from the 8th day, including weekends (Saturday and Sunday). 
    • Exotic currency pairs are alliable for 3-day Swap Free only.
    • After the Swap free period elapse, Swap fees charged and calculated Based on lot size and applied daily.
      • Deductions appear in the trading history with the remark: MLN_MTWD

    Swap fee Deduction Example

    • A client opens a trade transaction on Monday, 16th December: 
    • From 16th to 22nd December, no swap fee will be charged.
    • On Monday, 23rd December: a daily fee is deducted based on the client's open positions.

    For example, if the client has an open position of 1 lot EUR/USD and the swap fee rate for EUR/USD is $2 per day, a $2 daily fee will be deducted starting from 23rd December onwards.

    Swap Free Application Procedure

    Step 1: User verification 

    • If Requester is a client → CS must check the green tick (authorized user) on Zendesk before proceeding with the request.
       
    • If the client reached out on live chat without green-tick, CS must advice the client to open a new live chat window once login to the client portal or send us an email from the registered email address. 
       
    • If the requester is a Sales (AM) → CS must ensure that the request is made for a client under the AM’s reporting line.
       
    • CSR needs to check in the CRM whether the requester is already registered or not for a swap-free account to avoid unnecessary escalation.
       
    • If the Client is from Thailand TIM TH region, please go to the Thai Exception Process handling

    Step 2: Ensure client is Eligible

    • CS to ensure the client’s religion. Once confirmed. escalate to the Reporting Team.
       
    • Indonesian clients, including some who are not Muslim, are granted special approval to activate swap-free status and also on ECN accounts.

    Step 3: Escalate to Reporting

    • CSR needs to collect the following information:
    • Client Full Name
    • Trading Account:(s) Number (Not Email)
    The swap‑free option is enabled per trading account, not globally. Clients must provide all trading account numbers they want to be swap‑free.
    • Account Balance. If no deposit has been made, please provide the potential deposit amount 
    • forward the ticket to reporting team
    Important! Ensure all required information is submitted together with the request to avoid delays in review and approval. Incomplete applications may not be processed.

    Step 4: Enabling Swap fee option

    • The Reporting Team will verify whether the request is a Muslim client.
       
    • The Reporting Team will record the request in the CRM under Activity Management > Free Swap Activities.

    Step 5: Notify the Client

    • The CSR must verify in the CRM whether the Swap Free feature has already been enabled. This can be done by navigating to Promotion > Swap Free Management. Enter the account number to check the status and confirm whether the Swap Free is enabled.

    • After the swap-free option is enabled, CSR should provide the requester with steps on how to activate the swap-free option on their portal.

    Zendesk Macro 141: 

    "I'm happy to inform that your request for a swap-free account has been granted. You can now activate Swap free option on your trading accounts from the Profile: Log in to your Profile and navigate to Trading > click My Account > select the desired trading account > click the Apply for Swap-Free icon > click Apply to submit the request. Your selected trading account will then be converted to a swap-free account.

    You can check the step-by-step process with screenshots in the How to Activate Swap Free Option on Trading Accounts? article. 

    Note: If the client activated Swap free option on their trade account, the group would change from "S" to N automatically.

    • CSR to close the ticket with the theme "Global Promotion" and mark it as Solved

    Important Notes

    • Once the Risk team enables the swap‑free option for a client, the client may enable or disable the swap‑free option on any of their accounts 
    • There are no limits on the number of accounts a client may convert to swap‑free status. Clients may convert all eligible accounts.
    • To activate or deactivate the swap‑free option on an account, there must be no open positions.
    • Once a client is approved for swap‑free status, they will remain a swap‑free user permanently, unless they are found to be involved in arbitrage activities.
    • Indonesian clients, including some who are not Muslim, may be granted special approval to activate swap‑free status, including on ECN accounts.

    Exception Handling – Thailand

    • If the request is submitted by an AM or TL from Thailand, Tim BD approval is required.
    • If the request is submitted by Requests from IB / SIB / DC Clients:
    • CS must escalate the request to RP to verify in CRM that:
    • The client is linked to an approved free‑swap MIB
    • Free swap is applied only to the correct MH profile
    • Free swap is granted only to the profile under the approved “Free Swap” MIB
    • Do not apply swap‑free to any other profiles.

    ❌ CS must not redirect SIB/DC clients to Thai sales or account managers.

    Dashboard Activation Exception (Internal Only)

    Internal information! Do not disclose this system logic to sales teams or clients.

    Clients from Thailand can activate swap‑free directly from the dashboard for:

    • Standard account
    • Prime account 
    • ECN account
    • Not eligible by default
    • Exception only with John’s approval
    • Reporting must record ECN approval in CRM.

    Compliance and & Abusive Trading Behavior

    Swap‑free accounts are monitored for:

    • Arbitrage strategies
    • Swap exploitation
    • Systematic abuse

    If misuse is detected:

    • Swap‑free status may be revoked permanently
    • Normal swap conditions will apply immediately
    • No prior notice is required in cases of violation
    • All profits generated via abusive trading strategy can be deducted.
