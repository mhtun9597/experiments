    Changing Account's Trading Group

    Willow (OB)
    March 03, 2026 08:43 Updated
    The purpose of the Changing Trading Account Group SOP is to outline the step-by-step process for changing a client’s trading account group, including requirements, conditions, and actions for agents.

    There are two types of Trading account group changing.

    1. Changing from lower Spread to higher spread. 
    2. Changing from higher spread to lower spread
    • Step by Step Guide to change Trade Group of Account in CRM

    Process Flowchart

    1. Changing from lower spread to higher spread. 

    Important:
    Do NOT change the group directly. Ensure all positions are closed first.

    Step 1: Check IB Rebate

    • Refer to: Method for Checking the IB's Rebate
    • Condition 1: IB has rebate > Proceed to next step.
    • Condition 2: IB does not have rebate > Confirm with Sales. Once rebate is set, proceed.

    Step 2: Check Client’s Open Orders

    • Use MT Manager (Refer to: MT4/MT5 Manager Basic knowledge).
    • Condition 1: No open positions > Change account group.
    • Condition 2: Open positions > Inform Sales that request cannot proceed until positions are closed.

    Step 3: Change Account Group

    • Refer to: Steps to Change Trade Group of Account.

    Step 4: Record Update

    • Log the change in the Changing File.

    Step 5: Respond to Ticket

    • Use Macro Reply template.

    *Please follow the steps in the following link to respond to the ticket: Macro Reply.

    2. Changing from higher spread to lower spread.

    Note:
    When changing from higher spread to lower spread, positions do NOT need to be closed.

    Step 1: Check IB Rebate

    • Refer to: Method for Checking the IB's Rebate (OB SOP).
    • Condition 1: IB has rebate → Proceed.
    • Condition 2: IB does not have rebate → Confirm with Sales. Once rebate is set, proceed.

    Step 2: Change Account Group

    • Refer to: Steps to Change Trade Group of Account.

    Step 3: Record Update

    • Log the change in the Changing File.

    Step 4: Respond to Ticket

    • Use Macro Reply template.
