# MHTT Knowledge Base

## KB-001: Opening a Chart

### Overview

A chart displays historical and real-time market data for a selected trading instrument.

### Steps

1. Open the Symbol Search panel.
2. Search for the desired symbol.
3. Select the symbol from the search results.
4. A new chart will be opened in the active workspace.

### Notes

* Multiple charts can be opened simultaneously.
* Charts can be detached into separate panels depending on workspace settings.

---

## KB-002: Changing Timeframes

### Overview

Timeframes determine the period represented by each candle or bar on the chart.

### Steps

1. Open a chart.
2. Click the Timeframe selector on the top toolbar.
3. Select the desired timeframe.

### Available Timeframes

* 1 Minute
* 5 Minutes
* 15 Minutes
* 30 Minutes
* 1 Hour
* 4 Hours
* 1 Day
* 1 Week
* 1 Month

### Notes

Changing the timeframe does not affect open positions or orders.

---

## KB-003: Adding Indicators

### Overview

Indicators provide analytical calculations based on price and volume data.

### Steps

1. Open a chart.
2. Click the Indicators button.
3. Search for the indicator.
4. Select the indicator to add it to the chart.

### Common Indicators

* Moving Average
* RSI
* MACD
* Bollinger Bands
* Volume

### Notes

Multiple indicators may be applied to the same chart.

---

## KB-004: Drawing Trend Lines

### Overview

Trend lines are drawing tools used to visualize market direction.

### Steps

1. Open the Drawing Tools panel.
2. Select Trend Line.
3. Click the chart to define the starting point.
4. Click again to define the ending point.

### Notes

Trend lines can be moved, edited, locked, or deleted.

---

## KB-005: Creating Price Alerts

### Overview

Price alerts notify users when a market reaches a specified price level.

### Steps

1. Right-click a price level on the chart.
2. Select Create Alert.
3. Configure alert conditions.
4. Save the alert.

### Alert Types

* Price Above
* Price Below
* Price Crosses

### Notes

Alerts remain active until triggered or manually removed.

---

## KB-006: Creating a Watchlist

### Overview

Watchlists allow users to monitor selected symbols.

### Steps

1. Open the Watchlist panel.
2. Click Add Symbol.
3. Search for the desired instrument.
4. Select the instrument.

### Notes

A symbol may exist in multiple watchlists.

---

## KB-007: Saving a Workspace

### Overview

Workspaces store chart layouts and personal preferences.

### Steps

1. Open the Workspace menu.
2. Click Save Workspace.
3. Enter a workspace name.
4. Confirm the save operation.

### Notes

Saved workspaces can be loaded later from any supported device.

---

## KB-008: Placing a Market Order

### Overview

A market order executes immediately at the best available market price.

### Steps

1. Open the Order Panel.
2. Select Buy or Sell.
3. Enter the trade volume.
4. Click Submit Order.

### Notes

Execution price may differ slightly from the displayed price during volatile market conditions.

---

## KB-009: Placing a Limit Order

### Overview

A limit order executes only when the market reaches a specified price.

### Steps

1. Open the Order Panel.
2. Select Limit Order.
3. Enter volume.
4. Enter limit price.
5. Submit the order.

### Notes

Limit orders remain pending until filled, cancelled, or expired.

---

## KB-010: Modifying an Existing Order

### Overview

Pending orders can be modified before execution.

### Steps

1. Open the Orders panel.
2. Locate the target order.
3. Select Modify Order.
4. Adjust the required values.
5. Save changes.

### Editable Fields

* Entry Price
* Stop Loss
* Take Profit
* Expiration Time

---

## KB-011: Managing Open Positions

### Overview

The Positions panel displays all active trades.

### Available Actions

* Close Position
* Partial Close
* Modify Stop Loss
* Modify Take Profit

### Notes

Position updates are displayed in real time.

---

## KB-012: Locking Drawings

### Overview

Locked drawings cannot be accidentally modified.

### Steps

1. Select a drawing object.
2. Open Object Settings.
3. Enable Lock Object.

### Notes

Locked drawings remain visible but cannot be moved until unlocked.

## KB-016: Recovering a Workspace

### Overview

Workspace recovery allows restoration of previously saved layouts.

### Steps

1. Open Workspace Manager.
2. Select Recovery History.
3. Choose a recovery point.
4. Click Restore.

### Notes

Recovery points are automatically generated during workspace saves.
