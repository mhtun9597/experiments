    Sales Offboarding: Account Termination and Final Commission Payout Procedures

    Customer Assistant
    April 10, 2026 09:20 Updated

    The purpose of this SOP is to define the standardized process for offboarding resigned Account Managers and other Sales employees and ensuring accurate and timely final commission payouts. This procedure aims to maintain operational integrity, safeguard CRM data, and provide resigned employees with a clear and secure method to withdraw their final commissions within the stipulated timeframe.

    Scope

    This SOP applies to HR, Customer Service (CS), Onboarding, Data Analysis (DA), Risk Control (RC), Operations (OP), Funding, and Finance teams involved in the offboarding and commission payout process.

    Sales Offboarding Process

    Process Flowchart

    When HR receives a Sales resignation, they will send an email to the Customer Service team with the list of clients under the resigned Sales representative, to be transferred to a new assigned AM.

    Step 1: User verification

    CSR should ensure that the request received from HR before continuing to processing it.

    You can search requester name in Teams to confirm the position

    Step 2: CS Escalates to relevant teams

    • CSR updates the ticket Theme to "Sales Team Onboarding/Offboarding"
       
    • CSR escalates the ticket to Onboarding Team (Internal Note): For CRM offboarding and client reassignment.

    Macro 215: CS to Onboarding Team: Onboarding Team, I would appreciate it if you could check this ticket

    • CSR escalates the ticket to Data Analysis Team: For commission calculation.

    Macro 212: CS to Data Analysis Team: Data Analysis Team, I would appreciate it if you could check this ticket

    Step 3.1: OB Processes the ticket

    • Onboarding Team Restricts AM access to CRM to only wallet and withdrawal feature. And grants the 15-day CRM access. 
    CRM 2.0 handling note Since there is no offboarding function in CRM 2.0, the Offboarding (OB) team should create a record in the Change File and include the final date. Once the date is reached, the OB team must manually deactivate the AM’s account.
    • Reassigns clients to the new assigned AM according to the Process of Changing Account Manager
       
    • Onboarding Team Confirms completion and returns the ticket to CS

    Step 3.2: DA Processes the ticket

    • Data Analysis Team Actions Calculates final commissions. 
       
    • The Data Analysis Team escalates the ticket to Risk Control (RC) for approval. 
       
    • Once approved by RC, the Data Analysis Team escalates the ticket to Operations (OP) to credit the approved commission amount to the employee’s sales wallet.
       
    • After completion, the RC team confirms and returns the ticket to Customer Support (CS).

    Step 4: CS Solves the Ticket

    • CS informs HR that commission crediting is complete.
       
    • Marks ticket as Solved

    What happens after CS solves the ticket?

    • HR will inform the resigned Sales representative that they are eligible to withdraw their commission.
    • AM logs into CRM portal within the 7-day access window. During this period, access to all other CRM information will remain restricted. AM will have access to their own sales wallet and withdrawal request submission feature only.
    • AM will submit withdrawal application via standard process.
    • Funding Team processes withdrawal request according to usual withdrawal procedures. And performs fund remittance following standard remittance procedures.
    • On the 8th day after the initial request from the HR, the account will be automatically closed completely.
