    Trading Account Opening Procedure

    Willow (OB)
    April 14, 2026 07:20 Updated

    To outline the correct procedure for opening a namesake account while maintaining compliance, based on client type and hierarchy. 

    Purpose

    To provide a clear, compliant process for opening a namesake trading account in the CRM based on client type and hierarchy.

    Scope

    Applies to all staff handling requests via Zendesk for additional same-name accounts, ensuring adherence to internal and regulatory requirements.

    The Process Flowchart

     

    Step 1: User Verification

    1. Customer Support (CS) must verify that the requester is an authorized user by checking for a green tick in Zendesk.
    2. Do not share any personal or account information unless authorization is confirmed.
    3. If the green tick is missing:
      • Instruct the client to log in to the client portal.
         
      • Ask the client to initiate a new chat or email from their registered email address.
         
      • Resume the process only after authorization is verified.

    Step 2: Identify the Request Type

    If the requester is: Website DC
    • Inform the client that additional trading accounts can be opened directly from the dashboard
    • Provide step-by-step instructions How to open a Live Trading Account – MH Markets
    • End the process

    B. Account Manager (AM) Request

    • collect the account type and markup group. 
    • Proceed to Step 6 (Escalation to Onboarding)

    C. All Other Cases

    • Proceed to Step 4
       
    • A client who already has the same account type opened, then:
      • Inform the requester that additional trading accounts can be opened directly from the Dashboard.
         
      • Provide step‑by‑step guidance on how to open the account.
         
      • End the process.

    If the requester is an AM:

    • Collect all required information.
    • Proceed to Step 6 – Escalation to Onboarding (OB).

    In all other cases:

    • Proceed to Step 4.

    Step 4: Check Requester’s Account in CRM

    1. Review the requester’s profile and account setup in the CRM.
    2. Escalate to Onboarding (OB) if:
      • The requester already has the same account type on a different platform
        (e.g., Standard +2 MT4 exists and the client requests Standard +2 MT5).
      • The requester has an approved rebate scheme and link, but the trading account is not opened.
         
    3. In other scenarios such as:
      • DC under IB
      • DC under AM
      • SIB under MIB
      • IB under AM
        where the rebate scheme is not set up:
         
      • The requester must contact the assigned Account Manager (AM) / Upper Agent directly.
         
      • If the requester does not know who their AM is:
         

    Step 5: Contact Account Manager (AM)

    1. CS initiates a side conversation with the relevant AM (macro).
    2. Collect all necessary information required to proceed with account setup.
    3. Confirm rebate structure, hierarchy, and ownership details.

    Step 6: Escalation to Onboarding (OB)

    1. CS escalates the request to the Onboarding team with:
      • Client details
      • Account type and platform
      • Collected documentation and confirmations
    2. OB reviews and processes the account request.

    Step 7: Notify the Requester

    1. Once OB completes the action:
      • CS informs the requester of the outcome.
      • Share the trading account number (if applicable).
      • Provide any next steps or confirmation of readiness.
    2. Close the Zendesk ticket.

    • Ask the client to contact their superior agent or account manager.

    If NO (client does not know their superior agent or account manager)

    Situation 3: Requester is IB

    The process depends on whether the IB already has the requested account type available in a markup group.

    • If the IB already has the requested account type in a markup group and the promotion link is already set up, escalate the ticket to the OB Team.

    • If the requester does not know who their Superior Agent or Account Manager is, the CSR should contact the AM via side conversation and put the ticket on HOLD.
