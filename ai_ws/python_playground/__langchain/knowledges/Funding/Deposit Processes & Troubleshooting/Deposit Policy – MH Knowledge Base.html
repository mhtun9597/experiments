    Deposit Policy

    Customer Service
    July 31, 2025 10:56 Updated

    Deposit Time：

    The platform supports deposits 24/7. However, third-party deposit channels have varying service hours, and deposits may be adjusted based on channel availability during the early morning hours. For detailed information on deposit times, currencies, amounts, and procedures for each channel, please refer to the deposit instructions in the CRM. (In case of any special situations, please contact customer service promptly.) 

    *The daily exchange rate will be announced in internal group every morning 

    (If you notice any discrepancies between the system rate and the announced rate, please inform Summer.) 

     

    •  

    Deposit Amount and Deposit Currency：

    • Initial Deposit Requirement

    The required amount for a first-time deposit depends on the type of account selected.

    • Standard Account: USD 50
    • Prime Account: USD 100
    • ECN Account: USD 1,000
    • Cent Account: USD 10

    • Local Payment Deposit： 
    • Deposit Limits for Clients in China - Based on current regulatory requirements and channel configuration.


    - General Guidance:

    Deposit limits vary by channel. Please refer to each channel’s specific deposit instructions for details.

     

    • Standard Limits:

      • Minimum deposit: 3,000 RMB
      • Maximum deposit: 50,000 RMB

       

      Special Arrangements:

      • Now Pay - For amounts exceeding 300,000 RMB, advance coordination with channel is required.
      • BFT Channel supports deposits up to 800,000 RMB without prior notice.

      Large-value transactions initiated during midnight hours must be communicated in advance.

      • FaPay can accommodate up to 1 million RMB per transaction.
      • MTpay allows deposits up to 100,000 USD.

      *According to current regulations and channel usage. 

       

      1. For global customer’s single deposits, the minimum amount is $10 and the maximum deposit limits vary depending on the selected payment channel and are specified in the deposit page instructions and in accordance with the bank’s ceiling limits

       

      Deposit Procedure and Guidelines for Local Payment

      • Once a deposit channel is selected on the deposit page, corresponding instructions will be displayed—please review them thoroughly before proceeding.
      • Avoid including sensitive words/terms in payment remarks.
      • Third-party payments are not supported; any losses due to frozen channels from such actions will be borne by the customer.
      • Avoid initiating habitual transfers without first generating a deposit order, as the designated recipient account may change without prior notification.
      • For redirected channels, click “Paid” after completing the transaction.
      • For non-redirected channels, you must upload proof of payment upon completion.

       

       
      Digital Currency Deposit Limits (TRC20, ERC20, BEP20)
      Deposit restrictions apply—please consult specific channel guidelines for full details

      • Tether – Channel 4:

      Deposit restrictions apply： Per-transaction deposit limits

      • USDT TRC20: 6 USDT
      • USDT BEP20: 2 USDT
      • USDT ERC20: 21 USD

       

      • Other Channels:
        • Minimum Deposit : No requirement
        • Maximum Deposit: Unlimited.

       

      Deposit Procedure and Guidelines for Digital Currency  

      • Once a deposit channel is selected on the deposit page, corresponding instructions will be displayed—please review them thoroughly before proceeding.
      • After submission, you will be redirected to the payment page. Click on the USDT deposit address to copy it or scan the QR code.  (Please note USDT address is one-time use only.)
      • Please complete the payment within 15 minutes. The order is valid for 15 minutes.
      • Proceed to your personal e-wallet and transfer funds to the MH Markets e-wallet.
      • The payment amount must match the order amount exactly. Do not transfer more or less than the specified amount.

       

      VISA/Master Card Deposit Limits -
      Deposits via VISA and Master card are facilitated through MyFatoorah, a designated credit card service provider. This option is currently available exclusively to MAU clients.

      • Minimum Deposit: AED 182, aligned with the USD 50 CRM threshold
      • Maximum Deposit: Up to AED 1,000,000, subject to the individual cardholder’s limit

       

       

      Deposit Procedure and Guidelines for VISA/Master Card 


      Clients must provide a color copy of both the front and back sides of their card, ensuring the following details are visible:

      • Full name of the cardholder
      • Last 4 digits of your card number
      • Card expiry date

      Security Recommendations
      For security purposes, all digits except the last 4 on the front side of the card should be covered.
      The 3-digit CVV code on the back of the card must be covered (we do not require the CVV code).
      All other required details must be clear and visible.

      MyFatoorah Blacklisted Countries:
      OFAC Sanctioned Countries: (Afghanistan, Balkan Peninsula, Belarus, Burma, Central African Republic, Cuba, Congo , Ethiopia, Iran, Iraq, Lebanon, Libya, Mali, Nicaragua, North Korea, Somalia, South Sudan, Syria, Ukraine, Russia, Venezuela, West bank, Yemen)

       

      Wire Transfer Deposit Limits

      - Minimum Deposit: USD 1,000 (CRM Limit)
      - Maximum Deposit: USD 200,000 (CRM Limit)

      • Mashreq Bank Channel
      - Minimum Deposit: USD 1,000 (CRM Limit)
      - Minimum Deposit: AED 200 (CRM Limit)
      - Maximum Deposit: USD 100,000 (CRM Limit)

      Important Notes

      • Third-party deposits are strictly prohibited.
      • MH Markets does not accept deposits from the following jurisdictions:
