    How to Deposit via CN12 channel (ChipPay) - WeChat QR option

    Jason
    April 13, 2026 05:12 Updated

    At the moment clients looking for the WeChat QR option can use the CN12 channel. Below are instructions on how to deposit using WeChat QR code in CN12 channel (ChipPay).

    Step 1: Client Portal Deposit Dage

    Navigate to MH Market client portal Deposit page. 

    Step 2: Fill in Deposit Details

    1. Payment Currency choose CNY
    2. Payment Method choose Local Payment Methods
    3. Payment Channel choose 网银转账 CN12
    1. Choose the MT account you wish to deposit into
    2. Enter the amount you wish to deposit in USD currency 
    3. Remark any notes if necessary (Optional)
    4. Click the "Submit" button to proceed 

    Step 3: Verify Phone Number

    Enter the missing numbers from your registered phone number in the blank column, tick the agreement at the bottom to proceed.

    Step 4: Verify Your ID 

    1. Check and confirm your personal details are correct as per your MH Market profile.

       Upload a copy of your ID according to the instruction and press "Next“.

    2. Please read the contents in the declaration carefully. Once finished reading, please sign below and click "Confirm". 

    3. Read and scroll to the bottom to confirm Facial Verification agreement

       

    4. Follow the instructions and complete the required actions in sequence. These action may include nodding your head up and down, turning your head left and right, and opening your mouth. 
      Please do not wear sunglasses or a hat. Ensure you are in a well-lit environment, and avoid strong light exposure or reflections.

      Once completed, please remain still and wait for the verification process to finish. 

    5. If the system couldn't detect or is unable to access your device's camera, please use the QR code displayed to continue the verification on a device with a camera. 

    Steo 5: Choose Merchant

    If you wish to deposit using WeChat QR code, please choose Merchants that support WeChat payment method. 

    Step 6: Confirm the payment (*no visual guide at the moment)

    A QR code will appear where client can scan it with their WeChat app Scan function to confirm the payment.
