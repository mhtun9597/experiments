    Emergency Credit for Clients during Payment Channel Downtime or non-Working hours

    Arion
    April 09, 2026 03:47 Updated

    This SOP outlines the process for issuing emergency credit to Chinese clients when payment channels are unavailable during midnight hours and the client risks a Stop Out. Emergency credit supports the client's margin temporarily until normal deposit channels reopen.

    Note:
    This process is mainly designed for Chinese clients, but upon necessity can be applied to all clients, except clients under MAU regulation.

    Scope

    This procedure applies to:

    • Customer Service (CS), Account Managers (AM), Funding Team, Operations (OP) Team, Risk Control (RC) Team

    Exclusion:

    • MAU clients are NOT eligible for emergency credit.

    Eligibility Criteria

    A client is eligible for emergency credit ONLY if all conditions below are met:

    • Payment channels are unavailable during midnight, or Payment Channel is down
    Note:
    Funding Hours: 08:30–04:00 (GMT+8)
    • The client’s trading account is at risk of Stop Out.
    • The client has previous deposit history.

    Algorithm (Decision Flow)

    Process flowchart

    1. CS Initial Check
      • Confirm the payment channels are unavailable, 
      • Check if Trading account is at Stop Out risk.
        • If no Stop Out risk > kindly ask the client to wait for the payment method to go back online. 
      • Check if the client has previous deposits.
        • If no deposit history > tell the client to wait until payment method is back online
    2. Confirm Requested Credit Amount
      • $1–1,000: Client may request directly.
      • $1,001–3,000: AM must submit the request.
      • $3,001+: AM + approval from Annie/BD + escalate to RC
    3. Funding/OP Verification
      • CS checks with Funding if channels are available.
      • If Funding is offline or payment channels remain unavailable → escalate to OP with account details + requested amount.
      • OP verifies eligibility and applies the credit
    4. Post‑Credit Actions
      • CS checks MT Manager to confirm credit was applied.
      • Notify client: Deposit must be completed within 23 hours, or credit will be removed.
      • AM follows up next day.
