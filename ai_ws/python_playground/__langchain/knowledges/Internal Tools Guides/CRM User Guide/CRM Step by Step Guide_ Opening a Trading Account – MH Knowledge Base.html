    CRM Step by Step Guide: Opening a Trading Account

    KSLIM (OB)
    January 20, 2026 05:39 Updated
    If you are looking for the Opening a Trading Account Procedure, please refer to the Namesake Account Opening (OB SOP). In this we will review steps required for the opening trading account in CRM: 

    • TA Account Opening (with referrer / without referrer）

      • 1.1 Without referrer (Website Client)
      • 1.2 Clients with referrer (under sales or IB)
    • 2.1: Clients under sales
    • 2.2: Clients under IB

    TA Account Opening (with referrer / without referrer）

    If it's a TA client applying for an account:

    TA clients are divided into two types: with referrer (under sales or an IB) and 
without referrer (website clients or those not under any sales or IB).

    1.1 Without referrer (Website Client)

    • Clients (without Account Manager) from Website Announcement

    1.1.1) The website client solely intends to act as a trader.

    Step 1: Go to "Account Opening Application" and first confirm whether the client under referrer. If the client is without referrer, the "Parent" will display BCSD.GB(1SYO) or BCSD.CN(1RK3). At this point, we need to change the client to the appropriate RGM based on their nationality. Please refer to the information below for the change.

    Step 2: After confirming that the client is without referrer, we need to ensure that the "Account Name" and "Username" match (the client's name can be checked in the financial information).

    Step 3: Select the correct option based on the client's gender. (the client's gender can be checked in the financial information).

    Step 4: For clients without referrer, select "Website Client" for the deposit group (choosing "Website Client" as the deposit channel will prevent the client from making deposits).

    Step 5: Clients without a referrer can currently remain in the 'F' trading group.

    • Website Client Trading Group
       

    Step 6: After confirming that everything is correct, click on "TA Account Opening."

    Step 7: After completing the account opening, please record it in the designated Excel file:  AOR File.

    Step 8: Since we selected "Website Client" as the deposit channel for website clients, the client will not be able to make deposits. We need to send an email to the assigned BD/RGM so they can contact the website client in Ticket #47886.

    Step 9: If the BD/RGM reassigns the website client to a subordinate (TL/AM), we will wait for the assigned sales to respond via email before updating the client’s assignment.

    Step 10: Once confirmed, we will transfer the website client under their name but will not activate the deposit channel yet.

    Step 11: We will only proceed with activating the deposit channel after the sales confirms they have successfully contacted the client and requests activation.

    1.1.2) If the website client intends to become an Introducing Broker (IB)

    Situation A:

    The website client initially traded only as a trader and did not mention any intention of becoming an IB (Introducing Broker). After trading for a while, they expressed a desire to become an IB to receive rebates.

    The procedure to handle this case: 

    • Confirm whether the client has any deposit or withdrawal records.
    • Confirm whether the client has any trading history.

    After review, it has been confirmed that the client has previous deposit and withdrawal records and has also engaged in trading. Therefore, approval from the BD is required.

    Situation B:

    The website client initially traded only as a trader and did not mention any intention of becoming an IB (Introducing Broker). After trading for a while, they suddenly expressed a desire to become an IB to receive rebates.

    The procedure to handle this case: 

    • Confirm whether the client has any deposit or withdrawal records.

    After review, it has been confirmed that the client does not have any previous deposit or withdrawal records.
    Therefore, we will directly transfer the website client to the corresponding account manager according to the table below.

    1.2) Clients with referrer (under sales or IB)

    1.2.1: Clients under sales

    • According to the current workflow, all settings will be applied automatically in the client's application. We only need to update the account name and username to match the client's ID.

    However, we still need to ensure the following information is correct:

    Step 1: Go to "Account Opening Application" and first confirm whether the client has a referrer. If the sales are displayed in the "AM" column under "Parent," it indicates that the client is under a sales.

    Step 2: Next, ensure that the "Account Name" and "Username" match (the client's name can be viewed in the finance info).

    Step 3: Select the correct option based on the client's gender. (the client's gender can be checked in the financial information).

    Step 4: Since the client is under sales and not an IB, we do not need to check the rebate for the trading account applied in the MT group.

    Step 5: Check the client's nationality in the "Nation" column.

    Step 6: After confirming the nationality, select the appropriate option in the Deposit Group field. Please refer to this link for guidance: Change in Deposit Group

    Step 7: After confirming that everything is correct, click on "TA Account Opening."

    Step 8: After completing the account opening, please record it in the designated Excel file. AOR File

    1.2.2: Clients under IB

    Step 1: Go to "Account Opening Application" and first confirm whether the client has a referrer. If an IB is displayed in the "IB" column under "Parent," it indicates that the client is under an IB.

    Step 2: Next, ensure that the "Account Name" and "Username" match (the client's name can be viewed in the finance info).

    Step 3: Select the correct option based on the client's gender. (the client's gender can be checked in the financial information).

    Step 4: Check the client's nationality in the "Nation" column.

    Step 5: After confirming the nationality, select the appropriate option in the Deposit Group field. Please refer to this link for guidance: Change in Deposit Group

    Step 6: Since the client is under an IB, we need to check the trading account group applied in the MT group and confirm whether the client’s IB has set up the rebate for that group.

    *Please refer to the following link, 'Method for Checking the IB's Rebate (OB SOP),' for instructions.

    Step 7: After checking the IB's rebate settings, you can continue with the account opening process.

    Step 8: After confirming that everything is correct, click on "TAAccount Opening."

    Step 9: After completing the account opening, please record it in the designated Excel file. AOR File
